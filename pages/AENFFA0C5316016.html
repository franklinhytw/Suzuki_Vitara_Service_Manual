Clutch Pedal Assembly Removal and Installation
Removal
1)Remove steering column.  
2)Remove push rod from clutch pedal.  
3)Disconnect clutch pedal position (CPP) switch connector, and then remove clutch pedal position (CPP) switch from clutch pedal assembly, if necessary.
•CPP No.1 switch:  
•CPP No.2 switch:  
4)Remove clutch pedal assembly from vehicle body.
Installation
Reverse removal procedure noting the following points.
•Tighten each bolt and nut to specified torque.  
•Apply grease to push rod tip.
: Grease 99000–25100 (SUZUKI Silicone Grease)
•If additional lubrication is required, apply grease to specified position.  
: Grease 99000–25050 (SUZUKI Super Grease E)
