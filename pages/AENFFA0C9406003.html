Windshield Wiper Motor Removal and Installation
Removal
1)Remove wiper pivot caps (1) and wiper arm nuts (2), and remove windshield wiper arms (3).
2)Remove cowl top garnish.  
3)Remove wiper motor bolts (1) and remove wiper motor (2).
4)Disconnect wiper motor connector (3).
Installation
Reference: Windshield Wiper Motor Operation Inspection
Reverse removal procedure noting the following points.
•Tighten wiper motor bolts in numerical order (“1” – “3”) to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Wiper motor bolt (a): 8.3 N·m (0.85 kgf-m, 6.5 lbf-ft)
•Install windshield wiper arms (1) in positions shown in figure and tighten wiper arm nuts to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Wiper arm nut (a): 20 N·m (2.0 kgf-m, 15.0 lbf-ft)
