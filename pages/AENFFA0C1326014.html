1C
ECT Sensor Removal and Installation
Reference: ECT Sensor On-Vehicle Inspection:K14C
Removal
1)Disconnect negative (–) cable at battery.
2)Drain coolant.  
3)Disconnect connector from ECT sensor.
4)Remove ECT sensor (1) from thermostat case.
Installation
Reference: ECT Sensor Inspection:K14C
Reverse removal procedure noting the following points.
•Clean mating surfaces of ECT sensor and thermostat case.
•Check O-ring for damage and replace, if necessary.
•Tighten ECT sensor (1) to specified torque.
Tightening torque
ECT sensor (a): 12 N·m (1.2 kgf-m, 9.0 lbf-ft)
•Refill cooling system with coolant.  
•Check cooling system for leakage.
