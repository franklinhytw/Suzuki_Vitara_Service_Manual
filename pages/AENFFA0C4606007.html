Front Wheel Speed Sensor Removal and Installation
Reference: Front and Rear Wheel Speed Sensor On-Vehicle Inspection
Removal
1)Disconnect negative (–) cable at battery.
2)Hoist vehicle a little and remove wheel.
3)Remove front fender lining.  
4)Remove clips (1) and disconnect front wheel speed sensor connector (2).
5)Remove front wheel speed sensor bolt (3) and front wheel speed sensor harness bolt (4).
6)Remove front wheel speed sensor (5) from steering knuckle.
Installation
Reference: Front and Rear Wheel Speed Sensor Inspection
Reverse removal procedure noting the following points.
•Check that no iron powder is stuck to sensing area of wheel speed sensor (1).
•Tighten front wheel speed sensor bolt (2) and front wheel speed sensor harness bolt (3) to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Front wheel speed sensor bolt (a): 11 N·m (1.1 kgf-m, 8.5 lbf-ft)
Front wheel speed sensor harness bolt (b): 11 N·m (1.1 kgf-m, 8.5 lbf-ft)
•Check that there is no space between front wheel speed sensor and steering knuckle (4).
[A]: OK [B]: NG
•Install wheel.
