9E
Inner Rear View Mirror Removal and Installation
Removal
Equipped with auto dimming
1)Remove harness cover (1) and mirror stay cover (2).
2)Disconnect mirror connector (3).
3)Remove screw and inner rear view mirror (4) in arrow direction to remove it.
Not Equipped with auto dimming
1)Remove screw (1) and inner rear view mirror (2) in arrow direction.
Installation
Reverse removal procedure noting the following point.
•Tighten inner rear view mirror bolt to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Inner rear view mirror bolt: 3.5 N·m (0.36 kgf-m, 2.5 lbf-ft)
