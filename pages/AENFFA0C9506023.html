Power Door Mirror and Mirror Heater On-Vehicle Inspection (If Equipped)
Power Door Mirror Motor
1)Remove front door trim.  
2)Disconnect power door mirror connector (1).
3)Check battery terminals to power door mirror connector as shown in the following table and check operation of power door mirror motor.
If check result is not as specified in the following operation, replace power door mirror.
Power door mirror motor operation
Mirror Heater (If Equipped)
1)Remove front door trim.  
2)Disconnect power door mirror connector (1).
3)Check resistance between terminal “4” and terminal “8” of power door mirror connector.
If check result is not as specified, replace power door mirror.
Mirror heater resistance
Approx. 11.7 Ω (25 °C) (77 °F)
