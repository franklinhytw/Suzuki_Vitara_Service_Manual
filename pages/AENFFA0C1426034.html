1D
Timing Chain and Chain Tensioner Inspection
Reference: Timing Chain and Chain Tensioner Removal and Installation:K14C
Timing Chain Guide No.1
Check sliding surface of timing chain guide No.1 (1) for wear or damage.
If abnormality is found, replace timing chain guide No.1.
Timing Chain guide No.2
Check sliding surface of timing chain guide No.2 (1) for wear or damage.
If abnormality is found, replace timing chain guide No.2.
Timing Chain Tensioner
Check sliding surface of timing chain tensioner (1) for wear or damage.
If abnormality is found, replace timing chain tensioner.
Crankshaft Timing Sprocket
Check teeth of sprocket for wear or damage.
If abnormality is found, replace crankshaft timing sprocket.
Timing Chain
Check timing chain for wear or damage.
If abnormality is found, replace timing chain.
Timing Chain Tensioner Adjuster
Check that teeth (1) is free from damage.
If abnormality is found, replace timing chain tensioner adjuster.
