Inspection of Keyless Entry Control Module and Its Circuits
Voltage and Signal Check
Check voltage and/or pulse signal of keyless entry control module (3) using voltmeter (1) and oscilloscope (2).
NOTE:
•Check that battery voltage is 12 V or more.
•Voltage of terminal with asterisk (*) cannot be measured by voltmeter because it is pulse signal. Check it with oscilloscope if necessary.
[A]: Keyless entry control module connector (View: [a])
