9B
Front Fog Light Aiming Adjustment (Without DRL)
NOTE:
After any of the following services, aim fog lights.
•Front fog light bulb replacement
•Front fog light removal and installation
1)Adjust air pressure of all tires to specified value.
2)Have one person sit on driver seat.
3)Turn on fog lights.
4)Place screen (1) 3 m (9.8 ft.) ahead of vehicle.
5)Adjust with aiming screw so that bright / dark boundary is 60 mm (2.4 in.) below fog light center.
“a”: 3 m (9.8 ft.) 3. Downward adjustment
2. Upward adjustment
