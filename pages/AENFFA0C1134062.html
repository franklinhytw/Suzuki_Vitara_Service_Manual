P0641: Sensor Reference Voltage “A” Circuit / Open
Sensor power supply voltage “A” is out of specified range.
(1 D/C detection logic)
•Fuel pressure sensor and/or its circuit
•EGR-1 valve position sensor and/or its circuit
•EGR-2 valve position sensor and/or its circuit
•Boost pressure sensor and/or its circuit
•APP sensor and/or its circuit
•Inlet throttle valve position sensor and/or its circuit
•CMP sensor and/or its circuit
•A/C refrigerant pressure sensor and/or its circuit
•DPF® differential pressure sensor and/or its circuit
•Turbocharger actuator position sensor and/or its circuit
•ECM
P0651: Sensor Reference Voltage “B” Circuit / Open
Sensor power supply voltage “B” is out of specified range.
(1 D/C detection logic)
P0697: Sensor Reference Voltage “C” Circuit / Open
Sensor power supply voltage “C” is out of specified range.
(1 D/C detection logic)

•CMP sensor according to Step 2 – 5 of DTC P0340:D16AA.
