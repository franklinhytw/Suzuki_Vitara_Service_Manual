Outside Air Temperature Sensor Inspection
Reference: Outside Air Temperature Sensor Removal and Installation
Measure resistance of outside air temperature sensor using ohmmeter.
If check result is not as specified, replace outside air temperature sensor.
Outside air temperature sensor resistance
0 °C (32 °F): 4.943 – 6.120 kΩ
25 °C (77 °F): 1.604 – 1.800 kΩ
40 °C (104 °F): 0.845 – 0.980 kΩ
[A]: Resistance [B]: Temperature
