Water Pump Removal and Installation
Removal
1)Disconnect negative (–) cable at battery.
2)Drain coolant.  
3)Remove timing belt.  
4)Remove water pump (1).
Installation
Reference: Water Pump Inspection:D16AA
Reverse removal procedure noting the following points.
•Clean mating surface of water pump (1) and cylinder head.
•Use new gasket (2).
•Tighten water pump bolts (3) to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Water pump bolt (a): 26 N·m (2.7 kgf-m, 19.5 lbf-ft)
•Refill cooling system with coolant.  
•After installation, check each joint for leakage.
