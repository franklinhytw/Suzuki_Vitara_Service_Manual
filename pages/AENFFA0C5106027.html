ATF Cooler Hose Replacement
Rubber hoses for ATF cooler should be checked at specified interval. If replacing them, note the following.
•Replace clamps at the same time
•Insert hose as far as its limit mark
•Clamp clamps securely
•For K14C model, insert spacer (3) into new inlet hose (inlet to ATF cooler) (2) to position it as shown in figure.
[A]: M16A model 1. Outlet hose (Outlet from ATF cooler)
[B]: K14C model 2. Inlet hose (Inlet to ATF cooler)
“a”: 30 mm (1.1 in.)
