6B
Auto Steering Lock Inspection (Keyless Push Start Model)
1)Remove steering column lower cover.  
2)Measure voltage between terminals “1” – “4”, “2” – “5”, and “3” – “5”. If check result is not as specified, check auto steering lock wire harness for open or short and if auto steering lock wire harness is in good condition, replace auto steering lock (1).  
Auto steering lock unit terminal voltage
Terminal Normal voltage Condition
“1” – “4” 10 – 14 V
•Any door opened or closed with ignition “OFF”*
•Ignition “OFF → “ACC”*
0 – 1 V Other than above condition
“2” – “5” 10 – 14 V
•Any door opened or closed with ignition “OFF”*
•Ignition “OFF → “ACC”*
0 – 1 V Other than above condition
“3” – “5” 10 – 14 V
•Any door opened or closed with ignition “OFF”*
•Ignition “OFF → “ACC”*
0 – 1 V Other than above condition
NOTE:
*: Voltage can be checked only for a short time while locking or releasing of auto steering lock.
[A]: Steering lock connector (View: [a])
3)Install steering column lower cover.
