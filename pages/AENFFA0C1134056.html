P0520: Engine Oil Pressure Sensor / Switch Circuit
Measured voltage of oil pressure switch-1 signal circuit is higher than specified value when engine is not running.
(1 D/C detection logic but MIL does not light up)
•Oil pressure switch-1 and/or its circuit
•ECM

Oil pressure switch signal circuit check
1)When ignition is “OFF”, disconnect “D01” connector from ECM and oil pressure switch-1 connector.
2)Check for proper terminal connection to “D01” connector and oil pressure switch-1 connector.
3)If connections are OK, check the following points.
•Resistance of “A1” circuit: less than 1 Ω
•Resistance between “A1” circuit and ground: infinity
•Voltage of “A1” circuit: approx. 0 V (When ignition is “ON”)
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 3.
Repair or replace defective wire harness.
3
Oil pressure switch-1 check
1)Check oil pressure switch-1.  
Is check result OK?
Replace ECM and recheck DTC.  
Replace oil pressure switch-1.
