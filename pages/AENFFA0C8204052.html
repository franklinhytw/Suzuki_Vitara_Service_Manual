DTC Detecting Condition and Trouble Area
DTC detecting condition Trouble area
B1093-14: Side Impact Sensor Circuit Shorted to Ground (Driver Side)
Driver-side front side-impact sensor circuit has been shorted to ground.
•Driver-side and/or passenger-side front side-impact sensor circuit
•Driver-side and/or passenger-side front side-impact sensor
•SDM
B1093-15: Side Impact Sensor Circuit Shorted to Power Supply (Driver Side)
Driver-side front side-impact sensor circuit has been shorted to power supply or opened.
B1093-87: Side Impact Sensor Communication Data Invalid (Driver Side)
Driver-side front side-impact sensor has communicated with invalid data.
B1094-14: Side Impact Sensor Circuit Shorted to Ground (Passenger Side)
Passenger-side front side-impact sensor circuit has been shorted to ground.
B1094-15: Side Impact Sensor Circuit Shorted to Power Supply (Passenger Side)
Passenger-side front side-impact sensor circuit has been shorted to power supply or opened.
B1094-87: Side Impact Sensor Communication Data Invalid (Passenger Side)
Passenger-side front side-impact sensor has communicated with invalid data.
