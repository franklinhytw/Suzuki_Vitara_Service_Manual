DTC detecting condition Trouble area
B106C-1A: Curtain Air Bag Circuit Low Resistance (Driver Side)
Driver-side curtain air bag (inflator) module circuit resistance is less than specified range.
•Driver-side and/or passenger-side curtain air bag (inflator) module circuit
•Driver-side and/or passenger-side curtain air bag (inflator) module
•SDM
B106D-1A: Curtain Air Bag Circuit Low Resistance (Passenger Side)
Passenger-side curtain air bag (inflator) module circuit resistance is less than specified range.

DTC Troubleshooting
Step Action Yes No
1
Was “Air Bag Diagnostic System Check” performed?
Go to Step 2.
Go to Air Bag Diagnostic System Check.
2
Curtain air bag (inflator) module Check
1)Check that ignition is “OFF”.
2)Disconnect “L290” or “L291” connector.  
3)Connect “STEERING WHEEL” harness (1) of special tool (C), special tools (A) and (B) to “L290” or “L291” connector.
Special Tool
(A): 09932–76010
(B): 09932–78310
(C): 09932–75011
4)Set ignition “ON”.
5)Check for DTC.  
Is DTC B106C-1A or DTC B106D-1A indicated?
Go to Step 3.
Replace curtain air bag (inflator) module.  
3
Floor harness circuit check
1)Set ignition “OFF”.
2)Disconnect “L05” connector.  
3)Release shorting bar in SDM connector inserting release tool (B).
4)Check the following points.
•Resistance between “L05-17” and “L05-18” terminals (DTC B106C-1A): infinity
•Resistance between “L05-20” and “L05-19” terminals (DTC B106D-1A): infinity
Special Tool
(A): 09932–76010
(B): 09932–75100
Is check result OK?
Replace SDM and recheck DTC.  
Replace floor harness.
NOTE:
Upon completion of inspection and repair work, perform the following items.
•Reconnect all air bag system components and check that all components are properly mounted.
•Clear DTCs, referring to DTC Clearance if any DTC is detected.
•Repeat Air Bag Diagnostic System Check to check that the trouble has been solved.
