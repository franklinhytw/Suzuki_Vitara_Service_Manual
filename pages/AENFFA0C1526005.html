Oil Cooler Removal and Installation
Reference: Oil Cooler Components:K14C
Removal
1)Drain coolant.  
2)Remove turbocharger.  
3)Remove oil cooler (1) and oil cooler gasket.
Installation
1)Clean mating surface of cylinder block and oil cooler removing oil and coolant thoroughly.
2)Install oil cooler (1) with new gasket.
3)Tighten oil cooler bolts as follows.
a)Tighten oil cooler bolts lightly until they are seated.
b)Tighten oil cooler bolts to specified torque in numerical order (“1” – “5”).
Tightening torque
Oil cooler bolt (a): 20 N·m (2.0 kgf-m, 15.0 lbf-ft)
4)Install turbocharger.  
5)Refill cooling system with coolant.  
6)Check cooling system for leakage.  
7)Check engine oil level.  
8)Start engine and check that there is no leakage of engine oil and coolant.
