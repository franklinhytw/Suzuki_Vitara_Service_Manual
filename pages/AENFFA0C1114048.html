DTC Troubleshooting
Step Action Yes No
1
Was “Engine and Emission Control System Check” performed?
Go to Step 2.
Go to Engine and Emission Control System Check:M16A.
2
IMT valve actuator drive circuit check
1)With ignition “OFF”, disconnect “C01” and “E01” connectors of ECM and IMT valve actuator connector.
2)Check for proper terminal connection to “C01” and “E01” connectors and IMT valve actuator connector.
3)If connections are OK, check the following points.
•Resistance of “A1” and “A2” circuits: less than 1 Ω
•Resistance between each of “A1” and “A2” circuits and ground: infinity
•Resistance between “A1” circuit terminal and “A2” circuit terminal at IMT valve actuator connector: infinity
•Voltage of “A1” and “A2” circuits: approx. 0 V (When ignition is “ON”)
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 3.
Repair or replace defective wire harness.
3
IMT valve actuator check
1)Check IMT valve actuator.  
Is check result OK?
Replace ECM and recheck DTC.  
Replace IMT valve actuator.
