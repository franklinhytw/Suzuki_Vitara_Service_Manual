Sun Roof System Description
•Sun roof system consists of sun roof unit, sun roof motors with sun roof control modules and sun roof switch. The system can perform slide and tilt operations.
•Sun roof system is controlled by the sun roof control modules built in slide and tilt motors.
•Sun roof switch has “OPEN”, “CLOSE” and “PUSH TILT” positions. By operating this switch, the sun roof performs the actions shown in the following table depending on its position.
Sun roof operation table
Sun roof switch position Slide operation Fully closed Sunshade operation Tilt operation
Fully opened Partially opened Partially opened Fully opened Fully opened
OPEN No motion Slide-open Slide / sunshade-open Slide / sunshade-open Slide-open Tilt-down
PUSH TILT Tilt-up Tilt-up Tilt-up Tilt-up Tilt-up Tilt-down
CLOSE Slide-close Slide-close No motion Sunshade-close Sunshade-close Tilt-down
•Sun roof system has auto-slide and auto-tilt functions to fully open and fully close the sun roof by one switch operation.
•Sun roof system has anti-pinching function.
