DTC Detecting Condition and Trouble Area
DTC detecting condition Trouble area
P2288: Injector Control Pressure Too High
Measured fuel pressure in common rail is higher than specified value.
(1 D/C detection logic)
•Lack of fuel
•Fuel pressure sensor and/or its circuit
•Fuel flow actuator and/or its circuit
•Low fuel pressure circuit (fuel pressure regulator in fuel pump, fuel pump and/or its circuit, fuel hose, fuel pipe)
•High fuel pressure circuit (high pressure pump, common rail, high pressure pipe, fuel injector)
•ECM
P2289: Injector Control Pressure Too High – Engine Off
Measured fuel pressure in common rail is higher than specified value when engine is stopped.
(1 D/C detection logic)
