5A
Solenoid Harness with ATF Temperature Sensor Removal and Installation
Removal
1)Remove valve body assembly.  
2)Remove solenoid harness lock plate (1) and solenoid harness with ATF temperature sensor (2).
Installation
Reference: ATF Temperature Sensor Inspection
Reverse removal procedure noting the following points.
•Install solenoid harness lock plate properly.
•Use new solenoid harness connector O-ring (1) and new gasket (2).
•Apply ATF to O-ring and gasket.
