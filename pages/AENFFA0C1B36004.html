Exhaust Pipe and Muffler Removal and Installation
Reference: Exhaust System Components:D16AA
For replacement of exhaust pipe, hoist vehicle and observe WARNING under Exhaust System Components:D16AA and the following points.
•Tighten each bolt and nut to specified torque when reassembling.  
•After installation, start engine and check each joint of exhaust system for exhaust gas leakage.
