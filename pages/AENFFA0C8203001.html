Air Bag System Component, Wiring and Connector Location
NOTE:
•The figure shows LHD. For RHD, parts with (*) are installed on the opposite side.
•For discrimination of market code, refer to Identification Plate.
[A]: Without cruise control system 1. “AIR BAG” warning light 11. Front side-impact sensor (Market code P05, P06, P10, P30, P32, P35, P37, P38, P43,P48, P71, P85 and P96)
[B]: With cruise control system 2. Driver air bag (inflator) module 12. Front side-impact sensor (Market code P02, P22, P24, P54 and P90)
[C]: LHD model 3. Knee air bag (inflator) module (9 or 11-channel air bag system) 13. Rear side-impact sensor (8, 9 or 11-channel air bag system)
[D]: RHD model 4. Passenger air bag (inflator) module 14. “A/B” fuse (in J/B)
[E]: Connector number 5. Side-air bag (inflator) module (8, 9 or 11-channel air bag system) 15. SDM
[F]: Main harness 6. Front seat belt pretensioner 16. Passenger air bag deactivation switch (if equipped)
[G]: Floor harness 7. Rear seat belt pretensioner (11-channel air bag system) 17. “PASSENGER AIR BAG ON” indicator (if equipped)
[H]: Instrument panel harness 8. Curtain air bag (inflator) module (8, 9 or 11-channel air bag system) 18. “PASSENGER AIR BAG OFF” indicator (if equipped)
[I]: Front door wire (Driver-side) 9. Contact coil assembly    
[J]: Front door wire (Passenger-side) 10. Forward impact sensor
