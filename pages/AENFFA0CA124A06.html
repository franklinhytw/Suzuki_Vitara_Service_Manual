Adaptive cruise control at vehicle speed stored in memory cannot be resumed after adaptive cruise control was canceled by means other than CRUISE (main) switch
NOTE:
Adaptive cruise control system is controlled by the RBS control module communicating with ESP® control module, BCM, ECM, ENG A-STOP control module, steering angle sensor and combination meter through CAN communication. Before performing symptom diagnosis, check that no DTC is indicated in these control modules.
