Caution for Fastener
NOTICE:
Failure to use correct fasteners or to properly use fasteners can cause parts or system damage.
•When fasteners are removed, always reinstall them at the locations from which they were removed.
•All fasteners must be replaced with fasteners having the same part number. If a fastener of the correct part number is not available, a fastener of the same size having equal or higher strength may be used.
•This manual identifies and indicates special conditions for all non-reusable fasteners and fasteners requiring the use of thread-locking compound. Follow all instructions, including torque values.
