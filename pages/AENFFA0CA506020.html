Remote Controller Battery Replacement
1)Remove emergency key (4).
2)Remove upper case (1) using a flat-bladed screwdriver as shown in figure.
3)Remove printed circuit board (2) from lower case (3).
5. Shop rag
4)Remove battery (2) using a flat-bladed screwdriver (1) as shown in figure.
3. Shop rag
5)Place battery (lithium disc-type CR 2032 or equivalent) (1) so its “–” terminal faces “–” mark of the printed circuit board (2).
6)Install printed circuit board to lower case and then fit upper case securely.
NOTE:
Dispose of the used battery properly according to applicable rules or regulations. Do not dispose of lithium batteries with ordinary household trash.
