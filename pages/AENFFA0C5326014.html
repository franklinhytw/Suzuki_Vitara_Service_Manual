Clutch Operating Cylinder Assembly Inspection
Reference: Clutch Operating Cylinder Assembly Removal and Installation:K14C-6M/T Model
NOTICE:
If the clutch operating cylinder is washed, the grease contained within the bearing of clutch operating cylinder may be washed away, causing the bearing to seize up.
Do not wash the clutch operating cylinder.
Check clutch fluid leakage, spring for damage and bearing for smooth rotation. If malfunction is found, replace clutch operating cylinder assembly.
