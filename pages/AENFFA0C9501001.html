Power Window System Description
•Power window system consists of power window main switch, power window sub switches, driver power window regulator and other power window regulators.
•When driver power window switch is pushed down to the second step (auto switch and down switch are turned on), CPU in power window main switch turns on down relay, thereby causing door glass to move down fully automatically. After a certain period of time, CPU turns off the relay to discontinue auto down operation of door glass.
•When power window lock switch is pushed (lock position), ground line for other than driver power window circuit is opened and operations (up/down) of all windows other than driver power window are disabled.
