Outside Warning Buzzer Inspection
Reference: Outside Warning Buzzer Removal and Installation
1)Connect SUZUKI scan tool to DLC with ignition “OFF”.
2)Set ignition “ON”.
3)Select “Warning buzzer (Outside)” function in “Active Test” mode.
4)Check that outside warning buzzer sounds.
If check result is not as specified, check applicable circuit for open or short. If circuit is normal, replace outside warning buzzer and recheck it.
