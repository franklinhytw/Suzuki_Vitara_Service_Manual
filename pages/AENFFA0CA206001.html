10B
BCM Removal and Installation
LHD
Removal
1)Disconnect negative (–) cable at battery.
2)Remove driver side instrument panel undercover.
3)Remove nuts (1), and then remove BCM (2).
4)Disconnect connectors (3) and hood lock release cable from BCM.
Installation
Reverse removal procedure noting the following point.
•Tighten BCM nuts to specified torque.
Tightening torque
BCM nut: 5.5 N·m (0.56 kgf-m, 4.0 lbf-ft)
