B115A: Driving Modes Mismatched
Inconsistency between driving mode request signal and actual driving mode signal transmitted via CAN communication for specified time continuously.
•ECM
•TCM (A/T model)
•ESP® control module
•P/S control module
•4WD control module
•BCM
DTC Confirmation Procedure
1)Set ignition “ON” and leave ignition “ON” for 30 seconds or more.
