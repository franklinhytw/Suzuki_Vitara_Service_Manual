Yaw Rate / G Sensor Assembly On-Vehicle Inspection
Longitudinal G Inspection
1)Park vehicle on level surface with parking brake applied and fix wheels with chocks.
2)Connect SUZUKI scan tool to DLC (1) with ignition “OFF”.
Special Tool
(A): SUZUKI scan tool (Suzuki SDT-II)
3)Calibrate yaw rate / G sensor.  
4)Set ignition “ON” and select “DATA LIST” of SUZUKI scan tool. Refer to Operator’s Manual of SUZUKI scan tool for further details.
5)Check “G Sensor” under “DATA LIST” of SUZUKI scan tool in the following vehicle conditions.
•Level condition
•Front-up condition
•Rear-up condition
Replace ESP® hydraulic unit / control module if check result is not as specified.
Longitudinal G specification
Vehicle condition G sensor
Level condition 0 ± 0.1 G
Front-up condition 0.1 ± 0.1 G
Rear-up condition –0.1 ± 0.1 G
[A]: Front-up condition “b”: Approx. 380 mm (14.9 in.)
[B]: Rear-up condition 1. Safety stand
“a”: Approx. 370 mm (14.5 in.) 2. Chock

Yaw Rate Inspection
1)Connect SUZUKI scan tool to DLC (1) with ignition “OFF”.
Special Tool
(A): SUZUKI scan tool (Suzuki SDT-II)
2)Calibrate yaw rate / G sensor.  
3)Set ignition “ON” and select “DATA LIST” of SUZUKI scan tool. Refer to Operator’s Manual of SUZUKI scan tool for further details.
4)Check “Yaw Rate Sensor” under “DATA LIST” of SUZUKI scan tool in the following vehicle conditions.
•Parking condition
•Drive vehicle in right turning condition with steering wheel fully turned.
•Drive vehicle in left turning condition with steering wheel fully turned.
Replace ESP® hydraulic unit / control module if check result is not as specified.
Yaw rate specification
Vehicle condition Yaw rate
Parking 0 ± 4 deg/s
Right turning 45.0 ± 5 deg/s
Left turning –45.0 ± 5 deg/s
NOTE:
•Drive vehicle on a level surface at 10 km/h (6 mile/h).
•Turning radius is 5.2 m (17.1 ft.).
[A]: Right turning “a”: Approx. 5.2 m (17.1 ft.)
[B]: Left turning
