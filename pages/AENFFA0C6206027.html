6B
Pinion Shaft Rotation Torque Inspection
1)Remove steering gear case assembly.  
2)Remove rack boots.  
3)Check pinion shaft rotation torque with rack position centered using special tool.
Also check that rack as a whole moves smoothly.
If check result is not as specified, adjust pinion shaft rotation torque.
Special Tool
(A): 09944–16810
Rotation torque specification
Rotation angle Rotation torque
0° – 45° 0.8 – 2.0 N·m (0.08 – 0.20 kgf-m, 1.0 – 1.5 lbf-ft)
45° – end 3.0 N·m (0.31 kgf-m, 2.0 lbf-ft) max
