5B
Back Up Light Switch Removal and Installation
Removal
1)Remove battery and battery tray.  
2)Remove air cleaner suction hose and suction pipe.  
3)Support transaxle using chain hoist (3) or engine crane.
4)Remove engine left mounting No.1 bracket and No.2 bracket.  
5)Disconnect back up light switch connector (1).
6)Remove back up light switch (2).
Installation
Reference: Back Up Light Switch Inspection:5 M/T
1)Apply oil to new O-ring (1) and tighten back up light switch (2) to specified torque.
Tightening torque

2)Connect back up light switch connector.
3)Install engine left mounting No.1 bracket and No.2 bracket.  
4)Remove chain hoist from transaxle.
5)Install air cleaner suction hose and suction pipe.  
6)Install battery and battery tray.
