Brake Pedal Travel Inspection
NOTE:
The brake pedal-to-carpet clearance must be measured between the specified points that vary with vehicle specification. Select the appropriate measuring point (1) according to the figure below.
[A]: LHD-M/T model [C]: RHD-M/T model
[B]: LHD-A/T model and LHD-twin clutch system model [D]: RHD-A/T model and RHD-twin clutch system model
1)Start engine.
2)Depress brake pedal a few times.
3)With brake pedal depressed with force of about 300 N (30.6 kgf, 67.4 lbf), measure clearance “a” between brake pedal and carpet. If clearance “a” is less than specification, check the following points.
•Air in brake system:  
•Brake fluid leakage
•Length of brake booster push rod:  
Brake pedal to carpet clearance “a”
When pedal is depressed at 300 N (30.6 kgf, 67.4 lbf):
LHD: Over 49 mm (1.93 in.)
RHD: Over 59 mm (2.32 in.)
