DTC P0230
DTC Detecting Condition and Trouble Area
DTC detection condition Trouble area
P0230: Fuel Pump Primary Circuit
Any of the following conditions is met.
•Measured voltage of fuel pump relay drive circuit is higher than specified value.
•Measured voltage of fuel pump relay drive circuit is lower than specified value.
•Temperature of fuel pump relay drive circuit is out of specified range.
(1 D/C detection logic)
•Fuel pump relay (coil side) and/or its circuit
•ECM
