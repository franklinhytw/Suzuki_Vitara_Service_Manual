1F
Cooling System Draining
Before performing the following procedure, observe Precautions for Cooling System:K14C.
1)Remove radiator cap.
2)Hoist vehicle and remove engine undercover.
3)Insert hose (1) to service hole.
NOTE:
To avoid splashing coolant on radiator support member and front bumper lower absorber, use hose as specified below for installing to service hole.
Outside diameter “a”: 11 mm (0.43 in.)
Inside diameter “b”: 7 – 8 mm (0.28 – 0.31 in,)
Length “c”: More than 450 mm (17.72 in,)
4)Drain coolant from radiator drain plug (2).
5)Tighten drain plug securely.
