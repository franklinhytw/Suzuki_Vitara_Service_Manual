Fuel Leakage Check Procedure
After performing any service on fuel system, check that there is no fuel leakage as follows.
1)Set ignition “ON” for 3 seconds (to operate fuel pump) and then to “OFF”. Repeat this cycle of operation 3 or 4 times.
Fuel pressure should now be established in fuel system and felt by hand on fuel feed hose.
2)Check that there is no fuel leakage from any part of fuel system.
