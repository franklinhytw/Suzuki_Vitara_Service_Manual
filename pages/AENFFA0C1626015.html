Water Pump Removal and Installation
Removal
1)Disconnect negative (–) cable at battery.
2)Drain coolant.  
3)Remove water pump / generator drive belt.  
4)Remove water pump pulley (1).
5)Remove water pump (3) with generator bracket (2).
Installation
Reference: Water Pump Inspection:K14C
1)Clean mating surfaces of water pump and cylinder block.
2)Apply sealant to mating surface of water pump (1) as shown in figure.
“A”: Water tight sealant 99000–31250 (SUZUKI Bond No.1207F)
“a”: 3 mm (0.12 in.) “b”: 2 mm (0.08 in.)
3)Within 2 minutes after applying sealant, install water pump (1) with generator bracket (2) to cylinder block and tighten bolts and nuts to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Water pump bolt (a): 25 N·m (2.5 kgf-m, 18.5 lbf-ft)
Water pump nut (b): 25 N·m (2.5 kgf-m, 18.5 lbf-ft)
4)Install water pump pulley (3) and tighten bolts to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Water pump pulley bolt (c): 20 N·m (2.0 kgf-m, 15.0 lbf-ft)
5)Install water pump / generator drive belt.  
6)Connect negative (–) cable at battery.
7)Refill cooling system with coolant.  
8)Check cooling system for leakage.
