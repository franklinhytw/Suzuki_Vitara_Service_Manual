DTC Confirmation Procedure
B1513
1)Start engine and operate A/C system.
2)Change position of temperature selector from “LO” to “HI” gradually.
B1514
1)Start engine and operate A/C system.
2)Push “MODE” switch to change position from “FACE” to “DEF”.
B1531
1)Start engine and operate A/C system.
2)Push air intake switch to change position from “REC” to “FRE”.
