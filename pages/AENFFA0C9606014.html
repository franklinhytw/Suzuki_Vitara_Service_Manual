9F
Rear End Door Opener Switch Removal and Installation
Reference: Rear End Door Opener Switch On-Vehicle Inspection
Removal
1)Remove rear end door trim.  
2)Disconnect rear end door opener switch connector (1).
3)Remove rear end door license garnish (1).
4)Remove screws, and then remove rear end door opener switch (1).
Installation
Reverse removal procedure noting the following point.
•Tighten rear end door license garnish nuts to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Rear end door license garnish nut: 5.5 N·m (0.56 kgf-m, 4.0 lbf-ft)
