Steering angle sensor power supply circuit check
1)Check that ignition is “OFF”.
2)Disconnect steering angle sensor connector.
3)Disconnect “E08” connector.
4)Check for proper connection to “E08” connector.
5)If connection is OK, set ignition “ON”.
6)Measure voltage between “A1” circuit and ground.
Is it 10 – 14 V?
Go to Step 6.
Repair or replace “A1” circuit.
6
Steering angle sensor ground circuit check
1)Set ignition “ON”.
2)Measure resistance between “A2” circuit and ground.
Is it less than 1 Ω?
Go to Step 7.
Repair or replace “A2” circuit.
7
Steering angle sensor check
1)Connect steering angle sensor connector.
2)Check steering angle sensor.  
Is check result OK?
