P0630: VIN Not Programmed or Incompatible-ECM/PCM
VIN data is unregistered or inconsistent.
(1 D/C detection logic, monitoring once per D/C)
•No VIN or incorrect VIN
•ECM power and/or ground circuits
•ECM
DTC Confirmation Procedure
1)Set ignition “ON” for 2 sec.

Step Action Yes No
