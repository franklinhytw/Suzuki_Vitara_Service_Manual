Radiator Cooling Fan Assembly Removal and Installation
Reference: Radiator Cooling Fan Assembly On-Vehicle Inspection:D16AA
Removal
1)Disconnect negative (–) cable at battery.
2)Remove radiator.  
3)Remove cooling fan mounting bolts (1) and then remove radiator cooling fan assembly (2).
Installation
Reference: Radiator Cooling Fan Disassembly and Reassembly:D16AA
Reverse removal procedure noting the following points.
•Tighten cooling fan mounting bolts to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Cooling fan mounting bolt (a): 7.1 N·m (0.7 kgf-m, 5.5 lbf-ft)
•Connect connectors securely.
