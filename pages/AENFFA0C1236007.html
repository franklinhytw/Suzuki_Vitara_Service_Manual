A/F Sensor On-Vehicle Inspection
A/F Sensor Heater
1)Disconnect negative (–) cable at battery.
2)Disconnect A/F sensor connector.
3)Using ohmmeter, measure resistance between terminals “Heater +” and “Heater –” at sensor connector (1).
If resistance is not as specified, replace A/F sensor.  
NOTE:
Ambient temperature of sensor affects resistance largely. Check sensor heater is at reference temperature.
A/F sensor heater resistance
2.4 to 4.0 Ω at 25 °C (77 °F)
