P1335: ENG A-STOP DC-DC Converter “B” Performance
DC/DC converter input signal voltage does not change from the upper limit for more than specified time.
•DC/DC converter circuit / connector
•DC/DC converter
•ENG A-STOP control module
P1358: ENG A-STOP DC-DC Converter “B” Output Circuit Short to Ground
While ignition is “OFF”, DC/DC converter input signal voltage does not change from the lower limit for more than specified time.

DTC Troubleshooting
Step Action Yes No
1
DC/DC converter diagnosis signal circuit check
1)Set ignition “OFF”.
2)Disconnect DC/DC converter and “E24” connectors.
3)Check for proper terminal connection to DC/DC converter and “E24” connectors.
4)If connections are OK, check the following points.
•Resistance “B1” circuit: less than 1 Ω
•Resistance between “B1” circuit and ground: infinity
•Resistance between “B1” circuit terminal and other terminal at DC/DC converter connector: infinity
•Voltage of “B1” circuit: approx. 0 V (When ignition is “ON”)
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 2.
Repair or replace defective wire harness.
2
DC/DC converter power supply and ground circuit check
1)Check DC/DC converter power supply and ground circuits.  
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 3.
Repair or replace defective wire harness.
