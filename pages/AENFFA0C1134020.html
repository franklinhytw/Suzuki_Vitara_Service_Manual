DTC Detecting Condition and Trouble Area
DTC detection condition Trouble area
P0101: Mass or Volume Air Flow “A” Circuit Range / Performance
Any of the following conditions is met.
•Measured voltage of MAF sensor signal circuit is higher than specified value.
•Measured voltage of MAF sensor signal circuit is lower than specified value.
(3 D/C detection logic)
•MAF sensor and/or its circuit
•Air intake circuit
•ECM
