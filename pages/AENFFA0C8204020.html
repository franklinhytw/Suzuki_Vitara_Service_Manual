•Driver-side side-air bag (inflator) module
•Knee air bag (inflator) module
•Passenger-side side-air bag (inflator) module

•Driver-side curtain air bag (inflator) module
•Passenger-side curtain air bag (inflator) module
•Left-side and/or right-side rear seat belt pretensioner
•Those initiator circuits
B1033-00: Side Air Bag Deployment Record (Driver Side)
Deployment of driver-side side-air bag (inflator) module has been recorded in SDM.
B1034-00: Side Air Bag Deployment Record (Passenger Side)
Deployment of passenger-side side-air bag (inflator) module has been recorded in SDM.
B1035-00: Seat Belt Pretensioner Activation Record
Activation of front seat belt pretensioner has been recorded in SDM.
B1036-00: Curtain Air Bag Deployment Record (Driver Side)
Deployment of driver-side curtain air bag (inflator) module has been recorded in SDM.
B1037-00: Curtain Air Bag Deployment Record (Passenger Side)
Deployment of passenger-side curtain air bag (inflator) module has been recorded in SDM.
B1038-00: Knee Air Bag Deployment Record (Driver Side)
Deployment of knee air bag (inflator) module has been recorded in SDM.
B1039-00: Rear Seat Belt Pretensioner Activation Record
Activation of rear seat belt pretensioner has been recorded in SDM.
NOTE:
If DTC B1032-00, B1033-00, B1034-00, B1035-00, B1036-00, B1037-00, DTC B1038-00 or DTC B1039-00 is stored in SDM, it is not possible to clear DTC.
