Rear Planetary Gear Assembly and One-Way Clutch Assembly Disassembly and Reassembly
Reference: A/T Assembly Disassembly
Reference: A/T Assembly Reassembly
Reference: A/T Assembly Components
Reference: Front Planetary Gear Assembly and Input Shaft Components
Disassembly
1)Remove one-way clutch assembly (1).
2)Remove rear planetary carrier thrust washer No.1 (1).
3)Remove rear planetary carrier thrust washer No.2 (1).
4)Remove rear planetary sun gear No.1 (1), rear planetary sun gear No.1 thrust bearing (2) and rear planetary sun gear No.1 thrust bearing race (3).
5)Remove rear planetary sun gear No.2 (1), rear planetary sun gear No.2 thrust bearing race No.1 (2), rear planetary sun gear No.2 thrust bearing (3) and Rear planetary sun gear No.2 thrust bearing race No.2 (4).
Reassembly
Reverse disassembly procedure noting the following point.
•Install thrust bearing, thrust bearing race and thrust washer in correct direction referring to “Thrust Bearing, Thrust Bearing Race and Thrust Washer” under A/T Assembly Components.
