Enabling Air Bag System
1)Check that removed components are installed and/or disconnected connectors are connected properly.
2)Check that ignition is “OFF”.
3)Connect negative (–) cable at battery and/or install “A/B” fuse to J/B.  
4)Set ignition “ON”.
5)Check if “AIR BAG” warning light flashes for about 6 seconds and then comes off.
6)Perform Air Bag System Construction if defective.
