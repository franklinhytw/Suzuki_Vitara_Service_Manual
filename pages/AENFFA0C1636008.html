Radiator Cooling Fan Assembly On-Vehicle Inspection
Radiator Cooling Fan Operation
1)Connect scan tool to DLC (1) with ignition “OFF”.
Special Tool
(A): SUZUKI scan tool (Suzuki SDT-II)
2)Set ignition “ON” and clear DTC.  
3)Select “Radiator Fan” function in “Active Test”.
4)Check radiator cooling fan operation at low, mid and high modes.
5)If any abnormal condition is found, check cooling fan control system.  
Radiator cooling fan operation
  Fan motor Sub fan motor
OFF OFF OFF
Low mode Low speed Low speed
Mid mode High speed OFF
High mode High speed High speed
Radiator Cooling Fan Motor
1)Disconnect fan motor connectors.
2)Connect fan motor connector and battery as shown in figure.
3)Check radiator cooling fan motors rotate smoothly and properly. If any abnormality is found, replace corresponding radiator cooling fan motor.
