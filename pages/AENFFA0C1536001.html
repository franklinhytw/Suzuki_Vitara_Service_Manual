Engine Oil Level Check
1)Check engine oil level as follows:
a)Place vehicle on a level surface.
b)Warm up engine to normal operating temperature.
c)Stop engine and wait for 10 min.
d)Remove dipstick and wipe it with a clean lint-free cloth.
e)Reinsert dipstick fully.
f)Remove dipstick again and check oil level.
2)If oil level is above upper limit mark on the dipstick, proceed to either of the following steps depending on how much oil level exceeds upper limit.
a)If oil level is higher than upper limit by more than 15 mm (0.59 in.), perform the following steps:
i)Adjust oil level to upper limit mark on dipstick.  
ii)Perform After-sales regeneration.  
iii)If oil change request light blinks, replace engine oil and engine oil filter.  
b)If oil level is higher than upper limit by not more than 15 mm (0.59 in.), adjust oil level to upper limit mark on dipstick.  
3)If engine oil level is near lower limit mark on dipstick, add engine oil to upper level mark.
