1)Check that ignition is “OFF”.

Is “A/B” fuse in good condition?

4
SDM power supply circuit check
1)Check that ignition is “OFF”.
2)Disconnect “L04” connector.  
3)Set ignition “ON”.
4)Measure voltage between “L04-5” terminal and ground.
Special Tool
(A): 09932–76010
Is voltage 10 – 14 V?
Go to Step 5.
Replace floor harness.
5
Ground circuit check
1)Set ignition “OFF”.
2)Measure resistance between “L04-3” terminal and ground.
Special Tool
(A): 09932–76010
Is resistance 1 Ω or less?
SDM power supply circuit and ground circuit are in good condition.
Replace floor harness.
