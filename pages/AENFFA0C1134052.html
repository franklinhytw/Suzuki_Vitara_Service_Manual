DTC Detecting Condition and Trouble Area
DTC detection condition Trouble area
P0489: Exhaust Gas Recirculation “A” Control Circuit Low
Any of the following conditions is met.
•EGR-1 valve (+) drive circuit is open.
•EGR-1 valve (+) drive circuit is overload.
(3 D/C detection logic)
•EGR-1 valve and/or its circuit
•ECM
P0490: Exhaust Gas Recirculation “A” Control Circuit High
Any of the following conditions is met.
•EGR-1 valve (–) drive circuit is open.
•EGR-1 valve (–) drive circuit is shorted to power supply.
•EGR-1 valve (–) drive circuit is overload.
(3 D/C detection logic)
