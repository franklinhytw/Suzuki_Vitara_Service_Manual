9F
Precautions for Replacing Keyless Entry Control Module (Ignition Switch Model)
•When replacing the keyless entry control module, replace it with new one.
•After keyless entry control module is replaced with new one, perform “Immobilizer Key Registration” using SUZUKI scan tool.  
•When keyless entry control module and ECM are replaced at the same time, refer to ECM Registration:Ignition Switch Model for their registration.
•All of ignition keys must be ready for the registration.
