Front Bumper Disassembly and Reassembly
Reference: Front Bumper Removal and Installation
Disassembly
1)Remove front bumper license garnish (2) and front bumper lower garnish (4).
2)Remove front end side upper cover (3).
3)Remove front bumper cap (7).
4)Remove fog light cap (5) and DRL cap (6). (if equipped)
5)Remove front bumper pad (8) and front license plate retainer (9) if necessary.
[A]: Without front fog light or DRL [B]: Without LED DRL 1. Front bumper
Reassembly
Reverse remove procedure.
