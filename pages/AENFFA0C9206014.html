High Mount Stop Light Removal and Installation
NOTE:
High mount stop light bulb is LED.
Replace high mount stop light assembly if high mount stop light bulb is defective.
Removal
1)Remove high mount stop light nuts (1) and then remove high mount stop light.
2)Disconnect high mount stop light connector.
3)Disconnect rear washer hose (2).
3. Rear washer nozzle
Installation
Reverse removal procedure noting the following point.
•Tighten high mount stop light nuts to specified torque.
Tightening torque
High mount stop light nut (a): 2.0 N·m (0.20 kgf-m, 1.5 lbf-ft)
