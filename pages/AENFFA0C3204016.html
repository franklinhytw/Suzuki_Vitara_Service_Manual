Rear drive coupling unit circuit is shorted to power supply or ground.

DTC Confirmation Procedure
1)Set ignition “ON” for 1 minute.
DTC Troubleshooting
Step Action Yes No
1
Was “4WD control system check” performed?
Go to Step 2.
Go to 4WD Control System Check.
2
Rear drive coupling unit power supply circuit and rear drive coupling unit signal circuit check
1)Disconnect “L14” and rear drive coupling unit connectors with ignition “OFF”.
2)Check for proper connection to 4WD control module and rear drive coupling unit.
3)If connections are OK, check the following points.
•Resistance of “A1” and “A2” circuits: less than 1 Ω
•Resistance between “A1” circuit and ground: infinity
•Resistance between “A2” circuit and ground: infinity
•Resistance between “A1” circuit terminal and other terminal at rear drive coupling unit connector: infinity
•Voltage of “A1” and “A2” circuits: approx. 0 V (When ignition is “ON”)
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 3.
Repair or replace defective wire harness.
3
Rear drive coupling unit check
1)Check rear drive coupling unit.  
Is check result OK?
Substitute a known-good 4WD control module and recheck DTC.  
Replace rear drive coupling unit.
