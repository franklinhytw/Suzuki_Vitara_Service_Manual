Front Wheel Bearing Check
1)Check tightness of wheel bolts and retighten them to specified torque if necessary.  
2)Hoist vehicle.
3)Check wear of wheel bearing. When measuring thrust play, apply a dial gauge to front wheel as shown in figure.
Replace front wheel bearing if defective.
Front wheel bearing thrust play
Limit: 0.1 mm (0.004 in.)
4)Check wheel bearing noise and smooth wheel rotation by rotating wheel.
Replace front wheel bearing if defective.
