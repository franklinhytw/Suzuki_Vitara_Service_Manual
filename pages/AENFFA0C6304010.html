6
Combination meter power supply circuit check
1)Remove combination meter with ignition “OFF”.  
2)Check for proper connection to combination meter connector.
3)If check result is OK, set ignition “ON”.
4)Check voltage between “G240-3” terminal and “G240-5” terminal is battery voltage.
Is check result OK?
Replace combination meter and recheck DTC.
Go to Step 7.
7
Combination meter ground circuit check
1)Set ignition “OFF”.
2)Check resistance between “G240-5” terminal and ground is 1 Ω or less.
Is check result OK?
Repair or replace “A1” circuit.
Repair or replace “A2” circuit.
