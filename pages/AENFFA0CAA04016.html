C1638: RBS OFF Switch Circuit
RBS control module received ON signal of RBS OFF switch from BCM for specified time continuously.
•RBS OFF switch circuit / connector
•RBS OFF switch
•BCM

A1: RBS OFF switch signal circuit 2. BCM 4. CAN driver
DTC Confirmation Procedure
1)Set ignition “ON”.

Step Action Yes No

Was “RBS System Check” performed?

2
RBS OFF switch signal circuit check
1)Check that ignition is “OFF”.
2)Disconnect RBS OFF switch and “L01” connectors.
3)Check for proper terminal connection to RBS OFF switch and “L01” connectors.
4)If connections are OK, check the following points.
•Resistance of “A1” circuit: less than 1 Ω
•Resistance between “A1” circuit and ground: infinity
•Resistance between “A1” circuit terminal and other terminal at “L01” connector: infinity
•Voltage of “A1” circuit: approx. 0 V (When ignition is “ON”)
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 3.
Repair or replace defective wire harness.
3
RBS OFF switch check
1)Check RBS OFF switch.  
Is check result OK?
Replace BCM and recheck DTC.  
Replace RBS OFF switch.
