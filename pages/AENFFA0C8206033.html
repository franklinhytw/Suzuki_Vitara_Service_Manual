Passenger Air Bag (Inflator) Module Repair Harness Installation
If the harness (1) connected to passenger air bag (inflator) module (2) through passenger air bag (inflator) module connector (3) and instrument panel harness (4) are damaged, replace them with passenger air bag (inflator) module repair harness (5) according to the following procedure.
6. Floor harness
7. Insulating tape
8. Floor harness joint (yellow) connector with passenger air bag (inflator) module harness included in instrument panel harness
9. Floor harness joint (yellow) connector of passenger air bag (inflator) module repair harness
10. Passenger air bag deactivation switch (if equipped)
1)Disable air bag system.  
2)Replace passenger air bag (inflator) module.  
3)Remove glove box.
4)Disconnect passenger air bag (inflator) module connector (2) as follows.
a)Release locking slider (1) by sliding it in arrow direction “1”.
b)Disconnect passenger air bag (inflator) module connector by pulling it in arrow direction “2”.
5)Disconnect passenger air bag deactivation switch connector (1) (if equipped).
6)Cut off passenger air bag (inflator) module harness (1) included in instrument panel harness as shown, and then wrap the cut ends of the remaining harness with insulating tape to avoid interference with other parts.
[A]: LHD [D]: Passenger side
[B]: RHD 2. Passenger air bag deactivation switch connector (if equipped)
[C]: Driver side    
7)Fix passenger air bag (inflator) module repair harness (1) to instrument panel harness (2) with clamps (3).
8)Connect passenger air bag (inflator) module repair harness to floor harness (4).
[A]: LHD [C]: Driver side
[B]: RHD [D]: Passenger side
9)Connect passenger air bag (inflator) module repair harness to passenger air bag and passenger air bag deactivation switch (if equipped).
10)Enable air bag system.  
11)Check that no DTC is detected in SDM with ignition “ON”.
