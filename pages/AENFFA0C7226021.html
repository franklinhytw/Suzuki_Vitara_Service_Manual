7B
Expansion Valve Removal and Installation
Reference: HVAC Unit Components:Manual Type
Removal
1)Recover refrigerant from A/C system using recovery and recycling equipment referring to “Recovery” in Operation Procedure for Refrigerant Charge:Manual Type.
2)For HFC-134a model, remove suction pipe bolt (1), and then remove liquid pipe and suction pipe from expansion valve (2).
3)For HFO-1234yf model, remove inner heat exchanger pipe bolt (3), and then remove inner heat exchanger pipe from expansion valve.
4)Remove expansion valve bolts (4) and remove expansion valve.
Installation
Reference: Expansion Valve Inspection:Manual Type
Reverse removal procedure noting the following points.
•Apply compressor oil to O-rings of expansion valve and pipes.
Petrol model (HFC-134a): Compressor oil 99000–99095–00A (MITSUBISHI S10X)
Petrol model (HFO-1234yf): Compressor oil 99000–990B7–00A (Compressor oil (ND-OIL12))
Diesel model: Compressor oil 99000–990B7–00A (Compressor oil (ND-OIL12))
•Tighten expansion valve bolts (1) and suction pipe bolt (HFC-134a) (2) or inner heat exchanger pipe bolt (HFO-1234yf) (3) to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Expansion valve bolt (a): 3.5 N·m (0.36 kgf-m, 2.5 lbf-ft)
Suction pipe bolt (HFC-134a) (b): 11 N·m (1.1 kgf-m, 8.5 lbf-ft)
Inner heat exchanger pipe bolt (HFO-1234yf) (c): 11 N·m (1.1 kgf-m, 8.5 lbf-ft)
•Evacuate and charge A/C system referring to “Evacuation” and “Charge” in Operation Procedure for Refrigerant Charge:Manual Type.
