Fail-Safe Table
When any of the following DTCs is detected, 4WD control module enters the fail-safe mode as long as the failure condition associated with the DTC continues. The fail-safe mode is canceled when 4WD control module detects normal condition.
DTC DTC name Fail-safe operation
C1212   ECM Mismatch —
C1240   4WD Control Module Power Supply Circuit Malfunction 4WD control module stops power supply to rear drive coupling unit. Vehicle operates as 2WD.
C1243   Internal Circuit Malfunction Of 4WD Control Module Other than EEPROM error
•4WD control module stops power supply to rear drive coupling unit. Vehicle operates as 2WD.
EEPROM error
•4WD control module performs control with fail-safe value.
C1250   Coupling Air Temperature Sensor Open 4WD control module stops power supply to rear drive coupling unit. Vehicle operates as 2WD.
C1251   Coupling Air Temperature Sensor Short
C1252   Coupling Assembly Open
C1253   Coupling Assembly Short
C1260   Transfer Oil Temperature Sensor Open
C1261   Transfer Oil Temperature Sensor Short
C1270   CAN Invalid Data from BCM 4WD control module does not accept request from BCM.
C1281   4WD Tire of Different Diameter Installed 4WD control module stops power supply to rear drive coupling unit. Vehicle operates as 2WD.
C1282   4WD Abnormal Heat Generation
U0073   Control Module Communication Bus Off —
U0100   Lost Communication With ECM 4WD control module performs control with fail-safe value.
U0101   Lost Communication With TCM 4WD control module performs control with fail-safe value.
