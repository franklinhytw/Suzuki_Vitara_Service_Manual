Description of Auto-Slide and Auto-Tilt Functions
Auto Slide Function
•Auto slide function can be activated as follows with ignition “ON”.
—Auto slide-open: Fully closed or partly opened sun roof opens fully by pressing sun roof switch “OPEN” a bit longer.
—Auto slide-close: Fully or partly opened sun roof closes fully by pressing sun roof switch “CLOSE” a bit longer.
•Sliding motion is suspended if either “OPEN”, “CLOSE” or “PUSH TILT” of the sun roof switch is pressed during auto slide operation.
Auto Tilt Function
•Auto tilt function can be activated as follows with ignition “ON”.
—Auto tilt-up: Fully closed or partly opened sun roof in tilt direction tilt-ups fully by pressing sun roof switch “PUSH TILT”.
—Auto tilt-down: Fully or partly opened sun roof in tilt direction tilt-downs fully by pressing sun roof switch “PUSH TILT”.
•Tilting motion is suspended if either “OPEN”, “CLOSE” or “PUSH TILT” of the sun roof switch is pressed during auto tilt operation.
