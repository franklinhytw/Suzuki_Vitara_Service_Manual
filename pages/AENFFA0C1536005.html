Oil Pressure Switch-1 Removal and Installation
Removal
Reference: Oil Pressure Switch-1 On-Vehicle Inspection:D16AA
1)Disconnect negative (–) cable at battery.
2)Disconnect oil pressure switch-1 connector.
3)Remove oil pressure switch-1 (1) from heat exchanger No.1.
Installation
Reverse removal procedure noting the following points.
•Check oil pressure switch-1 gasket for deterioration and damage. If defect is found, replace oil pressure switch-1.
•Tighten oil pressure switch-1 (1) to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Oil pressure switch-1 (a): 26 N·m (2.7 kgf-m, 19.5 lbf-ft)
•Check that there is no engine oil leakage.
