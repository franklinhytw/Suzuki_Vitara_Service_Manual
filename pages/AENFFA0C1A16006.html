Tensioner Pulley and Generator Bracket Removal and Installation
Removal
1)Remove generator.  
2)Remove tensioner pulley (1).
3)Remove generator bracket (2).
Installation
Reverse removal procedure noting the following points.
•Tighten generator bracket bolts in numerical order as shown in figure.
a.Temporarily generator bracket bolts in numerical order (“1” – “4”).
b.Tighten them to specified torque in the same manner as in Step a).
Tightening torque
Generator bracket bolt (with tensioner pulley)* (a): 25 N·m (2.5 kgf-m, 18.5 lbf-ft)
•Tighten tensioner pulley bolt to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Tensioner pulley bolt (b): 45 N·m (4.6 kgf-m, 33.5 lbf-ft)
