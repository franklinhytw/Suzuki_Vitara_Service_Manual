1)Check that ignition is “OFF”.
2)Check for proper connection “L05” and “G168” connector.

4)Disconnect “G168” connector.  
5)Release shorting bar in SDM connector inserting release tool (B).
6)Check the following points.
•Resistance between “L05-32” and “G168-1” terminals: 1 Ω or less
•Resistance between “L05-31” and “G168-2” terminals: 1 Ω or less

NOTE:
Upon completion of inspection and repair work, perform the following items.
