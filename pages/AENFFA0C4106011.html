Brake Light Switch Inspection
Continuity Inspection
Reference: Brake Light Switch Removal and Installation
Check brake light switch for continuity between terminals at each shaft condition.
If check result is not as specified, replace switch.
Lighting Inspection
Depress the brake pedal until brake light turns on and measure the brake pedal stroke to that point.
If the pedal stroke is not as specified, remove and reinstall brake light switch.
Brake pedal stroke until brake light turns on
“a”: 6 – 15 mm (0.24 – 0.59 in.)
