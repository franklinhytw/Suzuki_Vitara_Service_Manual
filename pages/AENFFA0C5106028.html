5A
ATF Cooler Removal and Installation
Removal
1)Remove engine undercover.
2)Drain coolant.
•M16A model:  
•K14C model:  
3)Drain ATF.  
4)Disconnect ATF cooler hoses (1).
5)Remove ATF cooler (2).
Installation
Reverse removal procedure noting the following points.
•Use new O-ring and then apply ATF.
•Tighten ATF cooler bolts to specified torque.
Tightening torque
ATF cooler bolt (a): 21 N·m (2.1 kgf-m, 15.5 lbf-ft)
•Refill cooling system.
—M16A model:  
—K14C model:  
•Check ATF level.  
•Check for ATF leakage after warming up A/T.
