Hill Descent Control Switch Inspection
Reference: Hill Descent Control Switch Removal and Installation
Check hill descent control switch (1) for continuity between terminals at each switch position.
If check result is not as specified, replace hill descent control switch.
NOTE:
For checking diode circuit, select diode range of circuit tester.
[A]: Without ENG A-STOP OFF switch
[B]: With ENG A-STOP OFF switch
