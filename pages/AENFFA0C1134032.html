DTC P0168
DTC Detecting Condition and Trouble Area
DTC detection condition Trouble area
P0168: Fuel Temperature Too High
Limited value of fuel quantity is higher than specified value which varies depending on fuel temperature and engine speed.
(1 D/C detection logic but MIL does not light up)
•Fuel temperature sensor and/or its circuit
•Fuel heater and/or its circuit
•Engine cooling system
•Engine oil insufficient
•ECM
