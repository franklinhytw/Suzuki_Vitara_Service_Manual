P1336: ENG A-STOP DC-DC Converter “B” Enable Circuit / Open
Monitored status of DC/DC converter output signal circuit is higher than specified value for specified time.
•DC/DC converter circuit / connector
•DC/DC converter
•ENG A-STOP control module
P1337: ENG A-STOP DC-DC Converter “B” Enable Circuit Low
Monitored status of DC/DC converter output signal circuit is less than specified value for specified time.
P1338: ENG A-STOP DC-DC Converter “B” Enable Circuit High
Monitored status of DC/DC converter output signal circuit is higher than specified value for specified time.

DTC Troubleshooting
Step Action Yes No
1
DC/DC converter ON signal circuit check
1)Set ignition “OFF”.
2)Disconnect DC/DC converter and “E24” connectors.
3)Check for proper terminal connection to DC/DC converter and “E24” connectors.
4)If connections are OK, check the following points.
•Resistance “A1” circuit: less than 1 Ω
•Resistance between “A1” circuit and ground: infinity
•Resistance between “A1” circuit terminal and other terminal at DC/DC converter connector: infinity
•Voltage of “A1” circuit: approx. 0 V (When ignition is “ON”)
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 2.
Repair or replace defective wire harness.
2
DC/DC converter check
1)Check DC/DC converter.  
Is check result OK?
Replace ENG A-STOP control module and recheck DTC.  
Replace DC/DC converter.
