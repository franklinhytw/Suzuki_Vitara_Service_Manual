8B
Passenger Air Bag Deactivation Switch Removal and Installation (If Equipped)
Removal
1)Remove glove box.
2)Disconnect passenger air bag deactivation switch connector (1).
3)Remove passenger air bag deactivation switch (1) while releasing the locks (2).
Installation
Reference: Passenger Air Bag Deactivation Switch Inspection (If Equipped)
1)Install passenger air bag deactivation switch (1) aligning its projection (2) to cutout (3) of instrument panel.
2)Connect passenger air bag deactivation switch connector.
3)Install glove box.
