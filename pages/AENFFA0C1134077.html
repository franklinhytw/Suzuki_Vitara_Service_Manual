P2031: Exhaust Gas Temperature Sensor Circuit (Bank1 Sensor2)
Difference between EGT sensor-1 signal and other than EGT sensor-1 signal is higher than specified value when engine is cold started.
(3 D/C detecting logic)
•EGT sensor-1 and/or its circuit
•EGT sensor-2 and/or its circuit
•EGT sensor-3 and/or its circuit
•EGT sensor-4 and/or its circuit
•ECM
P242B: Exhaust Gas Temperature Sensor Circuit Range / Performance (Bank1 Sensor3)
Difference between EGT sensor-2 signal and other than EGT sensor-2 signal is higher than specified value when engine is cold started.
(3 D/C detecting logic)
P246F: Exhaust Gas Temperature Sensor Circuit Range / Performance (Bank1 Sensor4)
Difference between EGT sensor-3 signal and other than EGT sensor-3 signal is higher than specified value when engine is cold started.
(3 D/C detecting logic)
P24C2: Diagnostic Fault Check During Cold Start Condition of Exhaust-Gas Temperatures
At least one of EGT sensor-1, EGT sensor-2, EGT sensor-3 and EGT sensor-4 operates in a different way from the others.
(3 D/C detecting logic)
