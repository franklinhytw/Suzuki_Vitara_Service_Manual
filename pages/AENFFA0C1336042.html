VDOP Solenoid Valve Removal and Inspection
Removal
1)Disconnect negative (–) cable at battery.
2)Disconnect VDOP solenoid valve connector (1).
3)Remove VDOP solenoid valve (2) and oil seal from oil pump.
Installation
Reverse removal procedure noting the following points.
•Apply engine oil to new oil seal lip.
•Tighten VDOP solenoid valve bolt to specified torque.
Tightening torque
VDOP solenoid valve bolt (a): 8.0 N·m (0.82 kgf-m, 6.0 lbf-ft)
