3C
Transfer Intermediate Shaft Removal and Installation
Reference: Transfer Dismounting and Remounting
Reference: Transfer Assembly Components
Removal
1)Remove oil seal (1) and circlip (2) from transfer right case (3).
2)Tap transfer intermediate shaft (1) from transfer left case (2) side with plastic hammer (3) and remove it together with bearing.
4. Transfer right case
3)Remove snap ring (1) from transfer intermediate shaft (2).
4)Remove transfer intermediate shaft bearing (5) from transfer intermediate shaft using bearing puller (3) and hydraulic press (4).
Installation
1)Install transfer intermediate shaft bearing (1) to transfer intermediate shaft (2) using special tool and hydraulic press.
Special Tool
(A): 09913–84510
2)Install new snap ring (1) to transfer intermediate shaft (2).
3)Install transfer intermediate shaft to transfer from transfer right case (3) side.
4. Transfer left case
4)Install new circlip (1) to transfer right case (2).
5)Apply grease to lip of new oil seal (1).
“A”: Grease 99000–25011 (SUZUKI Super Grease A)
6)Using special tool and hammer, install oil seal in transfer right case (2).
Transfer intermediate output oil seal installing depth
“a”: 0.5 – 1.5 mm (0.02 – 0.05 in.)
Special Tool
(A): 09925–16010
