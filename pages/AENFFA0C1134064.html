NOTE:
For identification of each cylinder, refer to Statement on Cleanliness and Care:D16AA.
DTC Detecting Condition and Trouble Area
DTC detection condition Trouble area
P0671: Cylinder 1 Glow Plug Circuit / Open
Any of the following conditions is met.
•Glow plug No.1 circuit is open.
•Glow plug No.1 circuit is shorted to ground.
(1 D/C detection logic but MIL does not light up)
•Glow plug and/or its circuit
•Glow plug control module
•ECM
P0672: Cylinder 2 Glow Plug Circuit / Open
Any of the following conditions is met.
•Glow plug No.2 circuit is open.
•Glow plug No.2 circuit is shorted to ground.
(1 D/C detection logic but MIL does not light up)
P0673: Cylinder 3 Glow Plug Circuit / Open
Any of the following conditions is met.
•Glow plug No.3 circuit is open.
•Glow plug No.3 circuit is shorted to ground.
(1 D/C detection logic but MIL does not light up)
P0674: Cylinder 4 Glow Plug Circuit / Open
Any of the following conditions is met.
•Glow plug No.4 circuit is open.
•Glow plug No.4 circuit is shorted to ground.
(1 D/C detection logic but MIL does not light up)

DTC Troubleshooting
Step Action Yes No
1
Was “Engine and Emission Control System Check” performed?
Go to Step 2.
Go to Engine and Emission Control System Check:D16AA.
2
Glow plug drive circuit check
1)Check “C1”, “C2”, “C3” and “C4” circuits according to Step 7 of Glow Plug Operation Check:D16AA.
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 3.
Repair or replace defective wire harness.
3
Glow plug check
1)Check all glow plugs.  
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 4.
Replace glow plugs.  
4
Glow plug control module replacement
1)Replace glow plug control module.  
2)Clear DTC.  
3)Set ignition “OFF”.
4)Check DTCs.  
Is DTC P0671, P0672, P0673 or P0674 still detected?
Replace ECM and recheck DTC.
