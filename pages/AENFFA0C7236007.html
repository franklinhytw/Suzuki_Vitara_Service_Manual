7B
Desiccant Removal and Installation
M16A Model and D16AA Model
Refer to Desiccant Removal and Installation:Manual Type.
K14C Model
Removal
1)Recover refrigerant from A/C system referring to “Recovery” in Operation Procedure for Refrigerant Charge:Automatic Type.
2)Remove front bumper.  
3)For HFO-1234yf model, remove front bumper lower absorber (1).
4)Remove cap (1), and then remove O-rings (2) and desiccant (3) from receiver/dryer (4).
[A]: HFC-134a model [B]: HFO-1234yf model
Installation
Reverse removal procedure noting the following points.
•Do not take new desiccant out from plastic bag until just before inserting it into receiver/dryer.
•Apply specified compressor oil to O-rings.
Compressor oil 99000–990B7–00A (Compressor oil (ND-OIL12))
•Install new desiccant, new O-rings and new cap.
•Tighten cap (1) to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Cap (a): 18 N·m (1.8 kgf-m, 13.5 lbf-ft)
[A]: HFC-134a model [B]: HFO-1234yf model
•Evacuate and charge A/C system referring to “Evacuation” and “Charge” in Operation Procedure for Refrigerant Charge:Automatic Type.
