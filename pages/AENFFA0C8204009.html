Step Action Yes No

SDM power supply and ground circuit check
1)Check SDM power supply circuit and ground circuit.

4
CAN communication circuit check
1)Check CAN communication circuit between SDM and combination meter.
Is circuit in good condition?
Go to Step 5.
Repair defective wire harness.

Combination meter check
1)Replace combination meter.  
2)Set ignition “ON”.

Replace SDM and recheck “AIR BAG” warning light operation.  
End.
