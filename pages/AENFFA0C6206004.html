6B
Centering of Contact Coil Assembly
Reference: Contact Coil Assembly Removal and Installation
1)Remove steering wheel.  
2)Check that ignition is “OFF”.
3)Turn contact coil counterclockwise slowly with a light force till contact coil will not turn any further.
NOTE:
Contact coil can turn about 5 turns at the maximum, that is, if it is at the center position, it can turn about two and a half turns both clockwise and counterclockwise.
4)From the position of Step 3), turn contact coil assembly clockwise to place connector at top position within one rotation.
5)Turn rotation part (2) two turns clockwise to place connector (1) at top position. Check that triangle mark (4) on rotation part is aligned with that on stationary part (3) and flat cable (6) or orange roller (7) is visible in hole (5) as shown in figure.
