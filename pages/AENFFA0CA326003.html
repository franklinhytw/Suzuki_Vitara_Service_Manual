10C
ECM Registration
After ECM is replaced with new one, register keyless start control module information to the ECM using SUZUKI scan tool and security device.
•Perform “ECM Registration” in “Immobilizer” menu on SUZUKI scan tool.
Refer to Operator’s Manual of SUZUKI scan tool for further details.
NOTE:
•When ECM is replaced with new one, refer to “Procedure after ECM Replacement” or “Registration for ECM Replacement”.
