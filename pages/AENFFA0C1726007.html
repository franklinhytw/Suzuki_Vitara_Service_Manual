Fuel Pressure Sensor On-Vehicle Inspection
1)For M/T model, place gear shift lever in “Neutral”, apply parking brake and block drive wheels.
For A/T model, place select lever in “P”, apply parking brake and block drive wheels.
2)Connect SUZUKI scan tool to DLC (1) with ignition “OFF”.
Special Tool
(A): SUZUKI scan tool (Suzuki SDT-II)
3)Set ignition “ON” and select “Data List” mode.
4)Check “Fuel Rail Pressure” varies in response to “Vehicle condition”.
NOTE:
Right after engine stop, fuel pressure remains high.
  Vehicle condition Fuel pressure
1 IG switch: ON Approx. 500 kPa (5.1 kgf/cm2, 72.5 psi)
2 Engine: Idle speed after warming up Approx. 3000 kPa (30.6 kgf/cm2, 435.1 psi)
3 Engine: 2000 – 4000 rpm Approx. 3000 – 20000 kPa (30.6 – 203.9 kgf/cm2, 435.1 – 2900.8 psi)
