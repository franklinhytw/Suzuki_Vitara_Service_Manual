Oil Pump Inspection
Reference: Oil Pump Assembly Disassembly and Reassembly:K14C
Oil Seal
Check oil seal lip for defects and damage. Replace it if necessary.
Oil Pump
•Check outer rotor (1), inner rotor (2), rotor plate and timing chain cover for excessive wear or damage.
3. Front mark
•Check that relief valve (1) is not excessively worn or damaged and operates smoothly.
Radial clearance
Check radial clearance between outer rotor (1) and timing chain cover (2) using feeler gauge (3).
If clearance is out of specification, replace oil pump assembly.
Radial clearance between outer rotor and timing chain cover
Standard: 0.265 – 0.335 mm (0.0104 – 0.0131 in.)
Limit: 0.355 mm (0.0139 in.)
Side clearance
Using straightedge (1) and feeler gauge (2), measure side clearance.
If side clearance is out of specification, replace oil pump assembly.
Side clearance of oil pump inner rotor
Standard: 0.03 – 0.08 mm (0.0011 – 0.0031 in.)
Limit: 0.10 mm (0.0039 in.)
Relief valve spring free length and load
Check free length and load of relief valve spring as shown in figure. If any of measurement is smaller than specification, replace relief valve spring.
