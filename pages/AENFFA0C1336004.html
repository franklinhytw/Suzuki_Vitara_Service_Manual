1C
Injector Calibration Code Registration
After replacing one or more fuel injector(s) with new one, perform the following procedure.
1)When ignition is “OFF”, connect SUZUKI scan tool to DLC.
2)Set ignition “ON”.
3)Record injector calibration codes stamped on top of fuel injector.  
4)Select “Injector calibration code” in “Utility” menu.
5)Input injector calibration codes manually following instructions displayed on SUZUKI scan tool.
6)Check that injector calibration codes have been correctly input in ECM.
7)When ignition is “OFF”, disconnect SUZUKI scan tool from DLC.
