Charge warning light does not light with ignition “ON” and engine off
Trouble in charging system will show up as one or more of the following conditions.
Charging Warning Light Operation Failure
NOTE:
When ENG A-STOP system stops engine in normal condition, charge warning light does not light up.
Check “Charge Light” in Scan Tool Data to identify easily most possible cause of trouble. “Charge Light” indicates command signal of charge warning light from ECM to combination meter.
Condition Possible Cause Action
Charge warning light does not light with ignition “ON” and engine off
Blown fuse
Replace fuse.
Faulty combination meter
Replace combination meter.  
Faulty ECM
Check ECM.  
Faulty CAN communication line
Repair or replace CAN communication line.  
Faulty wiring
Repair wiring.
Faulty IC regulator or field coil
Repair or replace generator.
