Water Pump / Generator Drive Belt Tensioner Pulley Removal and Installation
Removal
1)Remove water pump / generator drive belt.  
2)Remove water pump / generator drive belt tensioner pulley.
Installation
Reverse removal procedure noting the following points.
•Tighten water pump / generator drive belt tensioner pulley bolt to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Water pump / generator drive belt tensioner pulley bolt (a): 45 N·m (4.6 kgf-m, 33.5 lbf-ft)
•Adjust belt tension.
