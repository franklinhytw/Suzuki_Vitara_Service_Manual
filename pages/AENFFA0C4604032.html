Step Action Yes No

Was “ESP® System Check” performed?

2
Back up light switch power supply circuit check
1)With ignition “OFF”, disconnect back up light switch connector.
2)Check for proper connection to back up light switch connector.
3)If connections are OK, set ignition “ON”.
4)Check that voltage between “A1” circuit and ground is approx. 12 V.
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 3.
Repair or replace defective wire harness.
3
Back up light switch signal circuit check
1)Set ignition “OFF”.
2)Disconnect “E336” and “E08” connectors.
3)Check for proper connection to “E336” and “E08” connectors.
4)If connections are OK, check the following points.
•Resistance of “A2” circuit: less than 1 Ω
•Resistance between “A2” circuit and ground: infinity
•Resistance between “A2” circuit terminal and other terminal at back up light switch, “E336” and “E08” connectors: infinity
•Voltage of “A2” circuit: approx. 0 V (When ignition is “ON”)
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 4.
Repair or replace defective wire harness.
