5B
Differential Side Oil Seal Replacement
1)Hoist vehicle and drain transaxle oil.  
2)Remove front drive shafts.  
3)Remove oil seal (1) using special tool.
Special Tool
(A): 09913–50121
4)Install new oil seal (1) to specified position using special tool.
NOTE:
•When installing oil seal, face its spring side inward.
•Check that oil seal is installed horizontally to surface of case after installing oil seal.
Special Tool
(A): 09944–88220
(B): 09944–66010
(C): 09924–74510
Distance from case surface to left oil seal
“a”: 1.0 – 2.0 mm (0.04 – 0.07 in.)
Distance from case surface to right oil seal
“b”: 8.5 – 9.5 mm (0.34 – 0.37 in.)
5)Apply grease to oil seal lip and at the same time check smoothness of area where drive shaft contacts oil seal.
“A”: Grease 99000–25011 (SUZUKI Super Grease A)
6)Insert front drive shafts.  
7)Pour transaxle oil.
