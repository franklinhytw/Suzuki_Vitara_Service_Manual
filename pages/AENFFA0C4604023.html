DTC Detecting Condition and Trouble Area
DTC detecting condition Trouble area
C102A: ABS Control Continuous Operation
ABS control is activated on all wheels continuously.		 ESP® control module
NOTE:
In emergency stop mode, when ESP® control module stops the control, the warning light will turn off but the DTC will remain as the present DTC during the current ignition cycle.
It is normal if DTC becomes history DTC when switching ignition from “OFF” to “ON”.
