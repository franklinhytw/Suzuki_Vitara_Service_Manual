B1511-L: Temperature Control Actuator (Position Sensor) and/or Its Circuit Malfunction
Signal voltage of position sensor in temperature control actuator is lower than specified value (0.1 V) for specified time continuously.
•Temperature control actuator circuit
•Temperature control actuator
•HVAC control module
B1512-L: Air Flow Control Actuator (Position Sensor) and/or Its Circuit Malfunction
Signal voltage of position sensor in air flow control actuator is lower than specified value (0.1 V) for specified time continuously.
•Air flow control actuator circuit
•Air flow control actuator
•HVAC control module
B1530-L: Air Intake Control Actuator (Position Sensor) and/or Its Circuit Malfunction

DTC Troubleshooting
NOTE:
•The following flow shows “DTC B1511-L: Temperature Control Actuator (Position Sensor) and/or Its Circuit Malfunction”.
•Check “DTC B1512-L / B1530-L” in the same manner as “DTC B1511-L” referring to the following table.
DTC Parameter Circuit
B1512-L Mode Pos Sensor “A3” and “B4” circuit
B1530-L R/F Pos Sensor ”A3” and “C4” circuit
Step Action Yes No

Scan tool parameter check
1)Check “Air Mix Pos Sen” using “Data list” mode on SUZUKI scan tool.  
Is voltage below 0.1 V?

Intermittent trouble.
2
Temperature control actuator position sensor signal circuit check
1)Disconnect temperature control actuator connector.
2)Check “Air Mix Pos Sen” using “Data list” mode on SUZUKI scan tool.  
Is voltage below 0.1 V?
Go to Step 3.
Replace temperature control actuator.  
3
Temperature control actuator position sensor signal circuit check
1)Set ignition “ON”.
2)Measure voltage between “A3” circuit terminal of temperature control actuator connector and ground.
Is voltage 5 V?
Go to Step 5.
Go to Step 4.
4
Temperature control actuator position sensor signal circuit check
1)Disconnect “G20” connector.
2)Check the following points.
•Resistance of “A3” circuit: less than 1 Ω
•Resistance of “A3” circuit between temperature control actuator and ground: infinity
Are resistances as specified?
Replace HVAC control module and recheck DTC.  
Replace instrument panel harness.
5
Temperature control actuator position sensor signal circuit check
1)Check that resistance between the “A3” and “A4” circuit terminals and ground is infinity.
Is resistance as specified?
Replace HVAC control module and recheck DTC.  
Replace instrument panel harness.
