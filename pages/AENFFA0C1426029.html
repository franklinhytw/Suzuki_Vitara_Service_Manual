OCV Removal and Installation
Reference: Timing Chain Cover Components:K14C
Removal
1)Disconnect negative (–) cable at battery.
2)Disconnect OCV connector (1).
3)Remove OCV (2) from timing chain cover.
Installation
Reference: OCV Inspection:K14C
Reverse removal procedure noting the following points.
•Apply engine oil to O-ring and carefully insert OCV to timing chain cover.
•Tighten OCV bolt (1) to specified torque.
Tightening torque
OCV bolt (a): 11 N·m (1.1 kgf-m, 8.5 lbf-ft)
