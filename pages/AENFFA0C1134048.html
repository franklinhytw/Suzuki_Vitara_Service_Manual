DTC detection condition Trouble area
P045A: Exhaust Gas Recirculation “B” Control Circuit
EGR-2 valve drive circuit is open.
(3 D/C detecting logic)
•EGR-2 valve and/or its circuit
•ECM
P045B: Exhaust Gas Recirculation “B” Control Circuit Range / Performance
EGR-2 valve drive circuit is overload.
(3 D/C detecting logic)
P045C: Exhaust Gas Recirculation “B” Control Circuit Low
EGR-2 valve (+) drive circuit is shorted to ground.
(3 D/C detecting logic)
P045D: Exhaust Gas Recirculation “B” Control Circuit High
EGR-2 valve (+) drive circuit is shorted to power supply.
(3 D/C detecting logic)
P1688: Exhaust Gas Recirculation “B” Control Circuit 2 Low
EGR-2 valve (–) drive circuit is shorted to ground.
(3 D/C detecting logic)
P1689: Exhaust Gas Recirculation “B” Control Circuit 2 High
EGR-2 valve (–) drive circuit is shorted to power supply.
(3 D/C detecting logic)
