6C
P/S Warning Light Check
1)Set ignition “ON” (without running engine) and check if the P/S warning light (1) comes on. If the light does not come on, go to P/S Warning Light Does Not Come ON with Ignition “ON” before Engine Starts of the diagnostic flows.
2)Start engine and check if the P/S warning light turns OFF. If light remains ON and no DTC is stored in P/S control module, go to P/S Warning Light Remains ON Steady or Blinks after Engine Starts for troubleshooting.
