Rear Differential Mounting Rear Bracket Removal and Installation
Removal
1)Remove muffler and suspend exhaust center pipe using a rope.

2)Support rear differential assembly with transmission jack (1).
3)Remove rear differential mounting rear bracket bolts (1), and then remove rear differential mounting rear bracket (2).
4)For D16AA-4WD model, remove mass damper (1) from rear differential mounting rear bracket (2), if necessary.
5)Remove rear differential mounting rear bracket bushing(s) as follows, if necessary.
a)Give matchmarks (1) on rear differential mounting rear bracket (2) for reinstallation of its bushing(s).

[B]: Vehicle right side    
b)Using special tools and hydraulic press (3), remove bushing(s) (1) from rear differential mounting rear bracket (2).

(B): 09944–78210

Installation
1)Install rear differential mounting rear bracket bushing(s) as follows, if removed.
a)Clean bushing hole(s) of rear differential mounting rear bracket.
b)Aligning rubber protrusions (4) of busing with matchmarks (1), place new differential mounting rear bracket bushing(s) (2) on rear differential mounting rear bracket (3) as shown.
[A]: Vehicle left side [B]: Vehicle right side
c)Using special tools and hydraulic press (3), install new rear differential mounting rear bracket bushing(s) (1) to rear differential mounting rear bracket (2).
Special Tool
(A): 09944–66040
(B): 09944–77030
(C): 09945–57910
2)Install mass damper (1) to rear differential mounting rear bracket (2), if removed.
Tightening torque
Mass damper bolt (a): 23 N·m (2.3 kgf-m, 17.0 lbf-ft)
3)Install rear differential mounting rear bracket (1) to vehicle and tighten its bolts (2) to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Rear differential rear bracket bolt (a): 80 N·m (8.2 kgf-m, 59.0 lbf-ft)
4)Lower transmission jack.
5)Install muffler.
