Quarter-Pillar Outer Panel and Side Body Outer Lower Extension Removal and Installation
NOTE:

•Front fender

Precautions
•Welding method and number of welding points are the same on both sides. This repair instruction shows procedure for left side.
•The dimensions shown in the figure are reference values when cutting replacement part placed on top of existing part. Determine the most appropriate cutting (or joint) line considering both size of replacement part and condition of existing part.
Removal
1)Mark at the shown position and cut quarter-pillar outer panel.
NOTICE:
You may accidentally cut the parts under the roof panel or the parts under the side sill outer panel when cutting the quarter-pillar outer panel.
Take sufficient care not to damage the parts under the quarter-pillar outer panel or the parts under the side sill outer panel when cutting the quarter-pillar outer panel.
2)Drill out welding spots.
3)Bend open metal tab (arrow) and remove quarter-pillar outer panel.

1: When heating around sealer application portion to detach body sealer, be sure that temperature of said portion does not reach 170 °C (338 °F).

3: This symbol indicates that quarter-pillar outer panel, rear wheel house outer panel and rear wheel house outer extension are replaced at the same time.
4)Drill out welding spots.
5)Remove side body outer lower extension, if necessary.
