DTC P0087 / P0088
DTC Detecting Condition and Trouble Area
DTC detection condition Trouble area
P0087: Fuel Rail / System Pressure – Too Low
Any of the following conditions is met.
•Measured fuel pressure in common rail is far below targeted fuel pressure for specified time.
•Calculate delivery fuel quantity is smaller than specified value.
•Fuel pressure in common rail is lower than specified value based on engine speed.
(1 D/C detection logic)
•Fuel pressure sensor and/or its circuit
•Low fuel pressure circuit (fuel pressure regulator in fuel pump, fuel pump and/or its circuit, fuel hose, fuel pipe)
•High fuel pressure circuit (high pressure pump, common rail, high pressure pipe, fuel injector)
•ECM
P0088: Fuel Rail / System Pressure – Too High
Any of the following conditions is met.
•Measured fuel pressure in common rail is far above targeted fuel pressure for specified time.
•Calculate delivery fuel quantity is smaller than specified value.
•Fuel pressure in common rail is higher than specified value.
(1 D/C detection logic)
