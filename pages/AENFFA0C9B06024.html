Removal

Installation
NOTE:
Before conducting any weld, check installation position and condition for installation part(s).
•Rear end door:  
•Windshield:  
1)Without sun roof model, conduct tack welding to roof panel No.1 member, roof panel No.2 member, roof panel No.3 member and roof panel No.4 member.
2)Apply body sealer.
[A]: Without sun roof model [B]: With sun roof model 1. Applied part of body sealer.
3)Install roof panel and 4 roof carrier brackets (roof carrier bracket model) or roof rail bracket (roof rail model).
4)Conduct resistance spot weld and plug weld.
: Conduct resistance spot weld as three panel thickness. [B]: With sun roof model [D]: Roof carrier bracket model
[A]: Without sun roof model [C]: Roof rail model 1. Press line
5)From cabin inside, drill ø 6.5 holes(1) and 9 x 10.5 oblong holes (2) to roof panel 10 places in total.
NOTE:
•As datum points, use existing holes in panels under the roof panel.
•While drilling, prevent iron powder from scattering.
•To make oblong hole, drill ø 9 hole first and process it to oval shape.
•After making holes, remove iron powder and burrs thoroughly.
•Rustproof holes.
