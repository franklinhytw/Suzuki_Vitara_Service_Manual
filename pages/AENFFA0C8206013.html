8B
Front Seat Belt Pretensioner Inspection
Reference: Front Seat Belt Pretensioner Removal and Installation
WARNING:
Failure to take proper precautions when checking the seat belt pretensioner can result in personal injury due to accidental activate of the seat belt pretensioner.
Never measure the resistance of the seat belt pretensioner.
Check seat belt assembly (retractor pretensioner) and if any of the following conditions is found, replace seat belt assembly (retractor pretensioner) with new one.
•Pretensioner being activated.
NOTE:
As retractor pretensioner is controlled with driver and passenger air bag, you should judge that pretensioner has been activated if air bag has deployed.
•Retractor pretensioner is damaged or a strong impact was applied to it.
•Connector being damaged.
