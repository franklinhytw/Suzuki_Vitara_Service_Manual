APP Sensor Assembly Removal and Installation
Reference: APP Sensor Assembly On-Vehicle Inspection:D16AA
NOTICE:
Failure to take proper precautions when handling the APP sensor assembly may lead to malfunction of the APP sensor.
•Do not drop or expose the APP sensor assembly to large shock. Any APP sensor assembly that has received large shock must be replaced.
•Keep the sensor section of the APP sensor assembly away from water.
Removal
1)Disconnect negative (–) cable at battery.
2)Disconnect connector from APP sensor assembly.
3)Remove APP sensor assembly.
Installation
Reverse removal procedure noting the following points.
•Tighten APP sensor assembly nut (1) and APP sensor bracket bolt (2) to specified torque.
Tightening torque
APP sensor assembly nut (a): 5.5 N·m (0.56 kgf-m, 4.0 lbf-ft)
APP sensor bracket bolt (b): 13 N·m (1.3 kgf-m, 9.5 lbf-ft)
[A]: LHD [B]: RHD
•Connect connector to APP sensor assembly securely.
