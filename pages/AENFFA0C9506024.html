9E
Door Mirror Removal and Installation
Removal
Reference: Power Door Mirror and Mirror Heater On-Vehicle Inspection (If Equipped)
1)Remove front door trim.  
2)Disconnect power door mirror connector.
3)Remove door mirror nuts (1) and remove door mirror assembly (2).
4)Remove door mirror (1).
5)Disconnect heater wires (2), if equipped.
6)Remove mirror visor cover (3) and door mirror turn signal light (4), if necessary.
5. Claw
6. Door mirror turn signal light connector (if equipped)
Installation
Reverse removal procedure noting the following points.
•Insert claws (2) securely when installing mirror (1).
•Pass mirror harness through door sash hole, insert locating projection (1) into another sash hole and fix claw (2) securely.
•Tighten door mirror nuts in numerical order (“1” – “3”) to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Door mirror nut* (a): 7.9 N·m (0.81 kgf-m, 6.0 lbf-ft)
•Check operation of door mirror after installation.
