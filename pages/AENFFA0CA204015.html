DTC Troubleshooting
Step Action Yes No
1
Air bag communication signal circuit check
1)Check that ignition is “OFF”.
2)Disconnect “L04” and “E05” connectors.
3)Check for proper terminal connection to “L04” and “E05” connectors.
4)If connections are OK, check the following points.
•Resistance of “A1” circuit: less than 1 Ω
•Resistance between “A1” circuit and ground: infinity
•Resistance between “A1” circuit terminal and other terminal at “L04” connector: infinity
•Voltage of “A1” circuit: approx. 0 V (When ignition is “ON”)
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 2.
Repair or replace defective wire harness.
2
BCM check
1)Check that ignition is “OFF”.
2)Connect “E05” connector to BCM.
3)Set ignition “ON”.
4)Check that voltage between “A1” and ground is battery voltage.
Is check result OK?
Replace SDM and recheck DTC.  
Replace BCM and recheck DTC.
