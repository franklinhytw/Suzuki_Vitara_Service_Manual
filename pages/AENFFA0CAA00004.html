Precautions for Removing / Installing and Replacing RBS Control Module
•After removing / installing or replacing RBS control module, perform Millimeter Wave Radar Aiming Adjustment.
•When installing RBS control module, check that the mounting portion is not damaged or deformed.
•Do not disassemble RBS control module. If disassembled, replace it.
•If RBS control module is exposed to a large shock, it will lose its original performance. Do not drop the control module. If the control module is exposed to a large shock, replace it even if the appearance is normal.
•A vehicle that was involved in an accident could be exposed to a large shock and might have lost its original performance. Perform “Millimeter Wave Radar Aiming Adjustment” for the accident vehicle and replace the RBS control module if any abnormality is found.
