Air Cleaner Filter Removal and Installation
Removal
Reference: Air Cleaner Components:D16AA
1)Remove air cleaner suction No.2 pipe (1).
2)Remove MAF sensor with IAT sensor-1 harness from air cleaner lower case clamp (2).
3)Open air cleaner upper case (3) by unhooking its clamps (4).
4)Remove air cleaner filter from air cleaner lower case.
Installation
Reference: Air Cleaner Filter Inspection and Cleaning:D16AA
Reverse removal procedure.
