5B
Manual Transaxle Oil Change
1)Before changing oil, stop engine and hoist vehicle horizontally.
2)With vehicle hoisted, check leakage. If leakage exists, correct it.
NOTE:
Whenever vehicle is hoisted for any other service work than oil change, also check for oil leakage.
3)Remove engine undercover.
4)Remove transaxle oil level / filler plug (1) and gasket (2).
5)Remove transaxle oil drain plug (3), and drain oil.
6)Apply sealant to thread of drain plug, and tighten it to specified torque.
“A”: Sealant 99000–31260 (SUZUKI Bond No.1217G)
Tightening torque
Transaxle oil drain plug (a): 21 N·m (2.1 kgf-m, 15.5 lbf-ft)
7)Pour new specified oil until a little oil flows from oil level / filler plug hole (4).
NOTE:
It is highly recommended to use “SUZUKI GEAR OIL 75W”.
Manual transaxle oil specification
: SUZUKI GEAR OIL 99000–22B27–036
: API GL-4
Manual transaxle oil capacity
Reference: 2.8 liters (5.92/4.93 US/Imp. pt.)
8)Install transaxle oil level / filler plug with new gasket, and then tighten it to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Transaxle oil level / filler plug (b): 27 N·m (2.8 kgf-m, 20.0 lbf-ft)
[a]: Temperature
