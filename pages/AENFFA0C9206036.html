9B
Headlight Leveling Switch Inspection (If Equipped)
Reference: Headlight Leveling Switch Removal and Installation (If Equipped)
Check resistance between terminals at each switch position.
If check result is not as specified, replace switch.
NOTE:
When measuring resistance between terminals “4” and “5”, set circuit tester in diode mode, because there is a diode between these terminals.
Switch position Terminal Resistance (Ω)
— 1 and 2 Approx. 4,600
0 2 and 3 Approx. 3,920
1 2 and 3 Approx. 3,240
2 2 and 3 Approx. 2,560
3 2 and 3 Approx. 1,880
4 2 and 3 Approx. 1,200
— 2 and 3 Continuity
