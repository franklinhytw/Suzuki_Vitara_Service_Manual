DTC U2001
DTC Detecting Condition and Trouble Area
DTC detecting condition Trouble area
U2001: LIN Lost Communication With Generator
Received data from generator via LIN is faulty.
(1 D/C detection logic but MIL does not light up)
•Generator LIN communication circuit
•Generator assembly
•ECM
Circuit Diagram
[A]: ECM connector (View: [a]) 1. Generator 3. ECM
A1: Generator LIN communication circuit 2. IC regulator 4. LIN driver
DTC Confirmation Procedure
1)Set ignition “ON”.
DTC Troubleshooting
Step Action Yes No
1
Was “Engine and Emission Control System Check” performed?
Go to Step 2.
Go to Engine and Emission Control System Check:D16AA.
2
Generator LIN communication circuit check
1)With ignition “OFF”, disconnect “E01” connector of ECM and generator connector.
2)Check for proper terminal connection to “E01” connector and generator connector.
3)If connections are OK, check the following points.
•Resistance of “A1” circuit: less than 1 Ω
•Resistance between “A1” circuit and ground: infinity
•Resistance between “A1” circuit terminal and other terminal at ECM connector: infinity
•Voltage of “A1” circuit: approx. 0 V (When ignition is “ON”)
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 3.
Repair or replace defective wire harness.
3
DTC recheck
1)Replace generator assembly.  
2)Perform “DTC Confirmation Procedure” and check DTC.
Is DTC U2001 still detected?
Replace ECM and recheck DTC.  
End.
