Brake Hose and Pipe Inspection
Perform this inspection where there is enough light and use a mirror if necessary.
•Check brake hoses and pipes for improper connections, leakage, crack, chafing and other damage.  
•Check that hoses and pipes are clear of sharp edges and moving parts.
Repair or replace any of these parts if necessary.
NOTICE:
Air in the brake system will cause poor braking performance and can lead to an accident.
After servicing the brake system, completely bleed all air out of the system.
