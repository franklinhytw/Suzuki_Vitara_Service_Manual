Inspection of RBS Control Module and Its Circuits
Voltage and Signal Check
1)Connect service wires (3) between RBS control module (1) and connector (2).
2)Check voltage and/or pulse signal of RBS control module using voltmeter (4) and oscilloscope (5).
NOTE:
Voltage of terminal with asterisk (*) cannot be measured by voltmeter because it is pulse signal. Check it with oscilloscope if necessary.
