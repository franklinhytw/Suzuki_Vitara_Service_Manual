Oil Pressure Switch-2 On-Vehicle Inspection
1)When ignition is “OFF”, connect SUZUKI scan tool to DLC.
Special Tool
: SUZUKI scan tool (Suzuki SDT-II)
2)Start engine and warm it up to normal operating temperature.
3)Select “Data List” mode and check oil pressure switch-2 as follows.
If faulty condition is found, replace oil pressure switch-2.
a)With engine at idle speed and accelerator pedal not depressed, check that “Oil Pressure switch 2” is OFF.
b)With accelerator pedal depressed and engine speed at 3,250 rpm, check that “Oil Pressure switch 2” is ON.
4)When ignition is “OFF”, disconnect SUZUKI scan tool from DLC.
