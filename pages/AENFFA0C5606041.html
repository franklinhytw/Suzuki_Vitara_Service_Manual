5F
Lower Countershaft Disassembly and Reassembly
Reference: Twin Clutch System Disassembly and Reassembly
Reference: Lower Countershaft Components
CAUTION:
Using an inappropriate hydraulic press or bearing puller could cause personal injury.
Use the hydraulic press and bearing puller which are capable of safely bearing a load of at least 5 tons (11,000 lbs).
NOTICE:
Supporting the gear on the concave side of the puller can cause damage to the gear.
Support the gear on the flat side of the puller.
Disassembly
NOTE:
The lower countershaft bolt is of a left-handed thread type pre-coated with adhesive.
Therefore, turn clockwise when removing it.
However, adhered bolt with adhesive requires higher loosening torque than specified tightening torque.
1)Hold parking lock wheel (1) with soft jawed vise and blocks (2) securely.
2)Remove lower countershaft bolt (3).
3)Remove lower countershaft left bearing (1) with 1st gear (2) using special tool and hydraulic press.
Special Tool
(A): 09925–98210
4)Remove 1st gear needle bearing (1).
5)Remove 3rd gear (1) with 1st & 3rd gear synchronizer sleeve & hub (2) and 1st gear spacer (3) using special tool and hydraulic press.
Special Tool
(A): 09925–98210
6)Remove 3rd gear needle bearing (1).
7)Remove parking lock wheel (2) with 3rd gear spacer (1) and washer (3) using special tool and hydraulic press.
Special Tool
(A): 09925–98210
8)Remove parking lock wheel spacer (1).
9)Remove 6th gear (1) with 6th gear synchronizer sleeve & hub (2) using special tool and hydraulic press.
Special Tool
(A): 09925–98210
10)Remove 6th gear needle bearing (1).
11)Remove circlip (1) using snap ring pliers (2), if necessary.
12)Remove lower countershaft right bearing (1) using hydraulic press.
13)Remove lower countershaft plug (1) from lower countershaft (3) using flat-bladed screwdriver (2) or the like and hammer.
Reassembly
1)To ensure lubrication of lower countershaft (1), blow air into oil holes (2) and check that they are free from any obstruction.
2)Install new lower countershaft plug (1) using special tool and hammer.
Special Tool
(A): 09925–58210
3)Install new lower countershaft right bearing (1) using special tools and hydraulic press.
Special Tool
(A): 09944–78210
(B): 09924–07710
(C): 09940–54910
(D): 09925–16010
4)Select new circlip as follows.
a)Measure clearance “a” between groove (1) of lower countershaft and lower countershaft right bearing (2) with feeler gauge (3).

18)Install new lower countershaft bolt (1) and tighten to specified torque.
NOTICE:
The lower countershaft bolt is pre-coated with adhesive. If the bolt is reused, it may work loose.
Use the new lower countershaft bolt.
Tightening torque
Lower countershaft nut (a): 110 N·m (11.2 kgf-m, 81.5 lbf-ft)
19)After replacing transaxle inner parts, perform TCM and Clutch Initialization.
