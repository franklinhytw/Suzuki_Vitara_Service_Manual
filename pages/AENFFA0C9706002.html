Front Seat Removal and Installation
Reference: Front Seat Components
Removal
1)Disable air bag system.  
2)Disconnect all connectors from front seat.
3)Remove 4 mounting bolts (2) and remove seat assembly (1).
4)Disassemble and repair parts if necessary.  
Installation
Reverse removal procedure noting the following points.
•Enable air bag system.  
•Tighten front seat mounting bolts to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Front seat mounting bolt: 23 N·m (2.3 kgf-m, 17.0 lbf-ft)
