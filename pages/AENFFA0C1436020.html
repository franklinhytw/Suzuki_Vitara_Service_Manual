Timing Belt, Belt Tensioner and Idler Pulley Removal and Installation
Reference: Timing Belt and Belt Tensioner Components:D16AA
NOTICE:
•Turning high pressure pump pulley counterclockwise may cause damage to the pump.
Never turn high pressure pump pulley counterclockwise.
•After timing belt is removed, if crankshaft or camshaft is turned independently, piston and valves may interfere and hit with each other and be damaged.
Avoid turning each of the camshaft or crankshaft alone while timing belt is removed.
•Bend or twist timing belt and contact of oil or water, etc. to timing belt may adversely affect the belt life.
Do not bend or twist timing belt and do not allow contact of oil or water, etc. to timing belt.
Removal
1)Remove engine assembly.  
2)Using special tool, lock flywheel (1).
Special Tool
(A): 09914–17930
3)Remove bolts (2) and remove crankshaft pulley (1).
4)Remove special tool from cylinder block.
Special Tool
(A): 09914–17930
5)Remove timing belt cover (1).
6)Remove engine right mounting bracket (2).
7)Remove EGR-2 valve bracket.  
8)Disconnect connector (1) from fuel pressure sensor.
9)Remove camshaft housing plugs (1).
10)Turning crankshaft clockwise, align camshaft housing plug holes (2) with camshaft gaps (3) and lock camshafts (4) inserting special tools (A) to plug holes as shown in figure.
NOTE:
For correct installation of timing belt, keep special tools inserted until timing belt is installed.
Special Tool
(A): 09917–68620
11)Loosen timing belt tensioner bolt (1) and remove timing belt (2).
12)Remove timing belt tensioner (3) and idler pulley (4).
Installation
Reference: Timing Belt Inspection:D16AA
1)Align crankshaft timing belt pulley as follows.
a)Remove oil pump bolt (1).
b)Install special tool to crankshaft timing belt pulley.
c)Rotate crankshaft slowly to fit locating dowel on crankshaft timing belt pulley into a hole in the tool.
Special Tool
(A): 09912–37910
d)Remove special tool from crankshaft timing belt pulley.
2)Install timing belt idler pulley (1).
Tightening torque
Timing belt idler pulley bolt (a): 26 N·m (2.7 kgf-m, 19.5 lbf-ft)
3)Install timing belt tensioner (2) as follows.
a)Fit slit (3) of timing belt tensioner to protrusion (4) on timing belt tensioner support.
b)Tighten timing belt tensioner bolt (5) by hand.
4)Install timing belt aligning matchmarks (1) on timing belt with matchmarks (2) on high pressure pump pulley, crankshaft timing belt pulley and camshaft timing pulley.
NOTE:
Set timing belt directing arrow mark (3) on the belt clockwise as shown in figure.
5)Install special tool to crankshaft timing belt pulley, rotate crankshaft slowly and fit locating dowel on crankshaft timing belt pulley into a hole in the tool.
Special Tool
(A): 09912–37910
6)Using a flat-bladed screwdriver (3), align movable index (1) of timing belt tensioner (2) to maximum tension position as shown in figure. And then, tighten timing belt tensioner bolt to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Timing belt tensioner bolt (a): 26 N·m (2.7 kgf-m, 19.5 lbf-ft)
7)Check matchmarks (1) on timing belt to matchmarks (2) on high pressure pump pulley, crankshaft timing belt pulley and camshaft pulley.
8)Remove special tools.
Special Tool
(A): 09917–68620
(B): 09912–37910
9)Turn crankshaft two revolutions clockwise.
10)Adjust timing belt tension as follows.
a)While keeping tension of timing belt using a flat-bladed screwdriver (4), loosen timing belt tensioner bolt (2).
b)Align movable index (3) of timing belt tensioner (1) to center of matchmark hole (5).
c)Tighten timing belt tensioner bolt to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Timing belt tensioner bolt (a): 26 N·m (2.7 kgf-m, 19.5 lbf-ft)
11)Turn crankshaft two revolutions clockwise.
12)Repeat Step 10) – 11).
13)Install new O-rings to camshaft housing plugs.
Tighten camshaft housing plugs to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Camshaft housing plug (a): 15 N·m (1.5 kgf-m, 11.0 lbf-ft)
14)Tighten new oil pump bolt to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Oil pump bolt: 9.0 N·m (0.92 kgf-m, 7.0 lbf-ft)
15)Install EGR-2 valve bracket.  
16)Install engine right mounting bracket (1), and tighten engine right mounting bracket bolts to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Engine right mounting bracket bolt (M10) (a): 50 N·m (5.1 kgf-m, 37.0 lbf-ft)
Engine right mounting bracket bolt (M8) (b): 26 N·m (2.7 kgf-m, 19.5 lbf-ft)
17)Install timing belt cover.
18)Using special tool, lock flywheel (1).
Special Tool
(A): 09914–17930
19)Install crankshaft pulley (1) and tighten bolts to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Crankshaft pulley bolt (a): 30 N·m (3.1 kgf-m, 22.5 lbf-ft)
20)Remove special tool from cylinder block.
Special Tool
(A): 09914–17930
21)Install engine assembly.
