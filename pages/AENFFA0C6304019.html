DTC C1164
DTC Detecting Condition and Trouble Area
DTC detecting condition Trouble area
C1164: CAN Invalid Data from BCM
Invalid data about steering assist torque request is received from BCM or steering assist torque request from BCM is received for more than 30 sec.
•CAN communication circuit
•BCM
•P/S control module (steering column assembly)
