Side Turn Signal Light Removal and Installation (If Equipped)
NOTE:
Side turn signal light is not repairable. Replace side turn signal light as a unit when bulb is blown or lens is broken.
Removal
1)Unlock a hook (3) by moving the side turn signal light in arrow directions “1”, “2” and “3” in sequence as shown in figure. Then remove side turn signal light (1).
2)Disconnect connector (2) from side turn signal light.
[A]: Left side turn signal light [a]: Vehicle forward
[B]: Right side turn signal light    
Installation
Reverse removal procedure.
