5A
Torque Converter Housing Disassembly and Reassembly
Reference: A/T Assembly Disassembly
Reference: A/T Assembly Reassembly
Reference: A/T Assembly Components
Disassembly
1)Remove oil reservoir lock plate (1) and lubrication tube (2).
2)Remove differential side oil seal (1) using a flat-bladed screwdriver or the like.
3)Remove pinion and counter driven gear right taper roller bearing outer race using special tools.
Special Tool
(A): 09944–96013
(B): 09942–15511
(C): 09921–26010
4)Remove differential right taper roller bearing outer race using special tools.
Special Tool
(A): 09944–96013
(B): 09942–15511
(C): 09921–26010
5)Remove torque converter housing plugs (1).
Reassembly
1)After applying ATF to new O-rings, fit them to housing plugs. Then tighten torque converter housing plugs to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Torque converter housing plug (a): 7.5 N·m (0.76 kgf-m, 5.5 lbf-ft)
2)Using special tools, install new differential right taper roller bearing outer race.
Special Tool
(A): 09924–74510
(B): 09944–88220
3)Using special tool, install new pinion and counter driven gear right taper roller bearing outer race.
Special Tool
(A): 09913–75510
4)Using special tool (A) (2WD model) or special tool (B) (4WD model), install new differential side oil seal to torque converter housing evenly at specified position shown in figure.
Special Tool
(A): 09945–56510
(B): 09951–18211
Differential side oil seal installing depth “a”
2WD model: 18.6 – 19.6 mm (0.74 – 0.77 in.)
4WD model: 24.6 – 25.6 mm (0.97 – 1.01 in.)
5)Apply grease to oil seal lip.
: Grease 99000–25030 (SUZUKI Super Grease C)
6)Install oil reservoir lock plate (1) and lubrication tube (2) by tightening their mounting bolts and clamp bolt to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Oil reservoir lock plate bolt (a): 5.4 N·m (0.55 kgf-m, 4.0 lbf-ft)
Lubrication tube clamp bolt (b): 5.4 N·m (0.55 kgf-m, 4.0 lbf-ft)
