Removal
1)Remove odd gear clutch assembly.  
2)Remove even gear clutch disc (1).
3)Remove even gear clutch assembly mounting bolts (1) evenly and gradually and remove even gear clutch assembly (2).
3. Bolt hole
Installation
Reference: Odd Gear Clutch Assembly and Even Gear Clutch Assembly Inspection
Reverse removal procedure noting the following points.
•Align a large dent portion (1) with even clutch control actuator pipe.
•Tighten even gear clutch assembly mounting bolts (1) evenly and gradually to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Even gear clutch assembly mounting bolt (a): 20 N·m (2.0 kgf-m, 15.0 lbf-ft)
•After replacing even gear clutch assembly, perform TCM and Clutch Initialization.
