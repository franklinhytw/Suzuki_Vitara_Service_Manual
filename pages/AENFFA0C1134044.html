P0404: Exhaust Gas Recirculation “A” Control Circuit Range / Performance
EGR-1 valve offset value is higher than specified value.
(3 D/C detection logic)
•EGR-1 valve position sensor and/or its circuit
•EGR-1 valve and/or its circuit

P0409: Exhaust Gas Recirculation Sensor “A” Circuit
Any of the following conditions is met.
•Measured voltage of EGR-1 valve position sensor signal circuit is lower than specified value.
•Measured voltage of EGR-1 valve position sensor signal circuit is higher than specified value.
(3 D/C detection logic)
•EGR-1 valve position sensor and/or its circuit
•ECM
