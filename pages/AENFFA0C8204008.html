Scan Tool Data Definition
System ID (4ch / 9ch / 11ch)
This parameter indicates number of initiator circuits.
Battery Voltage (V)
Power supply voltage is an analog input signal read by SDM.
Backup Voltage (V)
This parameter indicates capacity of backup capacitor installed to maintain ignition current (as much as possible) even when power supply to SDM that ignites inflator is shut off.
Driver Air Bag Initiator Resistance (Ω)
This parameter indicates resistance of driver air bag initiator circuit.
Passenger Air Bag Initiator Resistance (Ω)
This parameter indicates resistance of passenger air bag initiator circuit.
Driver-Side Seat Belt Pretensioner Initiator Resistance (Ω)
This parameter indicates resistance of driver-side front seat belt pretensioner initiator circuit.
Passenger-Side Seat Belt Pretensioner Initiator Resistance (Ω)
This parameter indicates resistance of passenger-side front seat belt pretensioner initiator circuit.
Driver-Side Side-Air Bag Initiator Resistance (Ω)
This parameter indicates resistance of driver-side side-air bag initiator circuit.
Passenger-Side Side-Air Bag Initiator Resistance (Ω)
This parameter indicates resistance of passenger-side side-air bag initiator circuit.
Driver-Side Curtain Air Bag Initiator Resistance (Ω)
This parameter indicates resistance of driver-side curtain air bag initiator circuit.
Passenger-Side Curtain Air Bag Initiator Resistance (Ω)
This parameter indicates resistance of passenger-side curtain air bag initiator circuit.
Knee Air Bag Initiator Resistance (Ω)
This parameter indicates resistance of the knee air bag initiator circuit.
Left-Side Rear Seat Belt Pretensioner Initiator Resistance (Ω)
