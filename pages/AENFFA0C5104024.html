DTC P0706 / P0707 / P0708
DTC Detecting Condition and Trouble Area
DTC detecting condition Trouble area
P0706: Transmission Range Sensor “A” Circuit Range / Performance
Faulty information is received from transmission range sensor. (Transmission range sensor is stuck.)
(1 D/C detection logic)		 TCM
P0707: Transmission Range Sensor “A” Circuit Low
Transmission range sensor signal circuit voltage is lower than specified value.
(1 D/C detection logic)
P0708: Transmission Range Sensor “A” Circuit High
Transmission range sensor signal circuit voltage is higher than specified value.
(1 D/C detection logic)
DTC Confirmation Procedure
NOTE:
Check that the following condition is met in this “DTC Confirmation Procedure”.
•DTC U0073 or U0100 is not detected.
1)Set ignition “ON”.
2)Shift select lever from P to M range.
