Cooling Fan Operation Description
Radiator cooling fan motor is controlled by ECM as follows.
[A]: High [C]: OFF
[B]: Low    
The following controls are also performed when A/C switch is ON.
Low speed:
Cooling fan operates at low speed when A/C refrigerant pressure is more than specified value of 1.1 MPa (11.2 kgf/cm2, 160 psi) or vehicle speed is lower than 30 km/h (19 mile/h).
High speed:
Cooling fan speed changes from low to high speed when A/C refrigerant pressure exceeds specified value of 1.5 MPa (15.3 kgf/cm2, 218 psi).
Fail-Safe
Cooling fan operates at high speed if malfunction related to ECT sensor is detected.
