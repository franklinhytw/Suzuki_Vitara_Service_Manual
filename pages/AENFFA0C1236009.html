Engine Oil Vapor Separator Removal and Installation
Removal
1)Remove EGR-1 valve with EGR-1 valve mounting.  
2)Remove starting motor.  
3)Disconnect breather hose (1) from engine oil vapor separator (2).
4)Remove engine oil vapor separator from cylinder block.
Installation
Reference: Engine Oil Vapor Separator Inspection:D16AA
Reverse removal procedure noting the following points.
•Use new O-ring.
•Connect breather hose securely.
•Fasten new breather hose clamp using special tool.
Special Tool
: 09943–57011
