EGT Sensor Inspection
Reference: EGT Sensor Removal and Installation:D16AA
1)Disconnect negative (–) cable at battery.
2)Disconnect EGT sensor connector.
3)Measure resistance between terminals of EGT sensor connectors.
If resistance is not as specified, replace EGT sensor.
EGT sensor resistance
EGT sensor-1, -2 and -3: 210 – 230 Ω at 25 °C (75 °F)
EGT sensor-4: 520 – 580 Ω at 25 °C (77 °F)
