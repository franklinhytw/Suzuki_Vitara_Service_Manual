B1062-14: Passenger Air Bag (1st stage) Circuit Shorted to Ground
Passenger air bag (inflator) module circuit has been shorted to ground.

•SDM

Step Action Yes No

2
Passenger air bag (inflator) module circuit check
1)Check that ignition is “OFF”.
2)Remove glove box.
3)Disconnect “G259” connector referring to Step 3) of “Removal” under Passenger Air Bag (Inflator) Module Removal and Installation.
4)Disconnect “L04” connector.  
5)Release shorting bar in SDM connector inserting release tool (B).
6)Check the following point.
•Resistance between each of “A3” and “A4” circuits and ground: infinity
Special Tool
(A): 09932–76010
(B): 09932–75100
Is check result OK?
Replace SDM and recheck DTC.  
Go to Step 3.
3
Floor harness circuit check
1)Disconnect “L378” connector.
2)Release shorting bar in SDM connector inserting release tool (B).
3)Check the following point.
•Resistance between each of “A3” and “A4” circuits and ground: infinity
Special Tool
(A): 09932–76010
(B): 09932–75100
Is check result OK?
Replace instrument panel harness.
Replace floor harness.
NOTE:
Upon completion of inspection and repair work, perform the following items.
•Reconnect all air bag system components and check that all components are properly mounted.
•Clear DTCs, referring to DTC Clearance if any DTC is detected.
•Repeat Air Bag Diagnostic System Check to check that the trouble has been solved.
