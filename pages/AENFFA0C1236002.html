1B
Turbocharger Actuator Inspection
Visual Check
Check turbocharger actuator for damage. If faulty condition is found, replace turbocharger with exhaust manifold.  
Operation Check
1)With ignition “OFF”, connect SUZUKI scan tool to DLC.
2)Set ignition “ON”, and select “Turbocharger Test” under “Utility” mode on SUZUKI scan tool.
3)Check operating sound from turbocharger actuator. If turbocharger actuator does not operate. Replace turbocharger with exhaust manifold.
