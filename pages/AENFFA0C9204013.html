DTC B1957 / B1958
DTC Detecting Condition and Trouble Area
DTC detecting condition Trouble area
B1957: Right Side Headlight Control Circuit / Open
Right side headlight unit diagnosis signal circuit voltage is higher than 1.5 V for specified time.
•Headlight unit (R) and/or its circuit
•Headlight LO relay
•Headlight auto leveling control module
B1958: Left Side Headlight Control Circuit / Open
Left side headlight unit diagnosis signal circuit voltage is higher than 1.5 V for specified time.
•Headlight unit (L) and/or its circuit
•Headlight LO relay
•Headlight auto leveling control module

DTC Confirmation Procedure
1)Set ignition “ON”.
2)Turn lighting switch to headlight position.
DTC Troubleshooting
NOTE:
This DTC troubleshooting explains the inspection for B1957: Right Side Headlight Control Circuit / Open. For B1958, perform the same inspection.
Step Action Yes No
1
DTC check
1)Clear DTC.  
2)Set ignition “OFF”.
3)Perform “DTC Confirmation Procedure” and recheck DTC.  
Is DTC B1957 still detected?
Go to Step 2.
Intermittent trouble.
2
Fuse check
1)Set ignition “OFF”.
2)Turn light switch to OFF position.
3)Check “H/L” fuse and “H/L R” fuse.
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 3.
Replace fuse and check for short circuit to ground.
3
Headlight LO relay check
1)Remove headlight LO relay.  
2)Check headlight LO relay.  
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 4.
Replace headlight LO relay.
4
Headlight unit diagnosis signal circuit check
1)Disconnect “E18” and “E155” connectors.
2)Check for proper terminal connections to “E18” and “E155” connectors.
3)If connections are OK, check “A1” circuit between “E18” connector and “E155” connector for open circuit, short to ground or short to power supply.
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 5.
Repair or replace defective harness and recheck DTC.  
5
Headlight unit replacement
1)Replace headlight unit.  
2)Clear DTC.  
3)Set ignition “OFF”.
4)Perform “DTC Confirmation Procedure” and recheck DTC.  
Is check result OK?
End.
Replace headlight auto leveling control module and recheck DTC.
