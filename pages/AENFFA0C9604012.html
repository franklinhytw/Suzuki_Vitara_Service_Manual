9F
DTC B1123
DTC Detecting Condition and Trouble Area
DTC detecting condition Trouble area
B1123: Backup Power Supply Malfunction
Backup power supply circuit voltage from “DOME” fuse is lower than specified value.
•“DOME” fuse
•Each system connected to “DOME” fuse
•Keyless entry control module
DTC Confirmation Procedure
1)Set ignition “ON”.
DTC Troubleshooting
Step Action Yes No
1
Fuse check
1)Check that ignition is “OFF”.
2)Check “DOME” fuse.
Is check result OK?
Replace keyless entry control module and recheck DTC.  
Replace “DOME” fuse and go to Step 2.
2
DTC check
1)Set ignition “ON”.
2)Check DTC.  
Is DTC B1123 still detected?
Go to Step 3.
End.
3
Each system check connected to “DOME” fuse
1)Check each system connected to “DOME” fuse, except keyless entry control module.
Is check result OK?
Replace “DOME” fuse and replace keyless entry control module, and recheck DTC.  
Repair or replace defective part and/or wire harness, and then replace “DOME” fuse and recheck DTC.
