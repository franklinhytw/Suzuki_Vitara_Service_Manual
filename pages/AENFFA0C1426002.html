1D
Air Cleaner Filter Removal and Installation
Reference: Air Cleaner Components:K14C
Removal
1)Remove engine cover as follows to avoid interference between the cover and MAF sensor with IAT sensor-1 harness.  
a)Release rear side fitting, pulling it up lightly.
b)Release front side fitting, pulling it up lightly.
c)Remove the engine cover, lifting it straight.
2)Disconnect waste gate control hose No.3 (1) from air cleaner cap (2).
3)Unhook air cleaner case clamps (3).
4)Open air cleaner cap, and then remove air cleaner filter.
Installation
Reference: Air Cleaner Filter Inspection and Cleaning:K14C
1)Install air cleaner filter to air cleaner case.
2)Fit tabs (1) of the cap with the case lower side hooks, and join air cleaner housing cap (2) with air cleaner case (3).
3)Hook air cleaner case clamps.
4)Connect waste gate control hose No.3.
5)Install engine cover.
