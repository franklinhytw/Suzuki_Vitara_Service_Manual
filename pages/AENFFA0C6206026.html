6B
Tie-Rod / Rack Boot Removal and Installation
Reference: Steering Gear Case Assembly Components
Removal
1)Remove steering gear case assembly.  
2)Remove tie-rod end.  
3)Remove band (2) and rack boot clip (1).
4)Remove boot (3) from tie-rod.
5)Unbend bent positions of tie-rod lock washer (1).
6)Hold steering rack end (4) using vise (2) with suitable spacers (3) as shown and remove tie-rod (5) from steering rack (6).
“a”: 5 mm (0.20 in.) “b”: 10 mm (0.39 in.) or more
Installation
Reference: Steering Rack Boot Inspection
1)Install tie-rod (1) and new tie-rod lock washer (3) to rack (2), and then tighten tie-rod to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Tie-rod (a): 88 N·m (9.0 kgf-m, 65.0 lbf-ft)
2)Bend lock washer (1) at 2 positions as shown in figure.
3)Apply grease to section “A” of tie-rod shown in figure. Position boot properly in grooves of steering gear case and tie-rod.
Check that boot is free from twist and dent.
“A”: Grease 99000–25050 (SUZUKI Super Grease E)
4)Fasten new band (1) using special tool, and install rack boot clip (2).
Special Tool
(A): 09943–55010
5)Install tie-rod end.  
6)Install steering gear case assembly.
