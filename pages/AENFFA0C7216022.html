7B
Rear Duct (If Equipped) Removal and Installation
Removal
1)Disable air bag system.  
2)Remove front seats.  
3)Remove floor console rear box and floor console front covers.  
4)Remove front side sill scuffs and dash side trims.  
5)Take off carpet till rear ducts are totally exposed.
6)Remove right and left rear ducts (1).
7)Remove center rear duct (2) if necessary.
Installation
Reverse removal procedure.
