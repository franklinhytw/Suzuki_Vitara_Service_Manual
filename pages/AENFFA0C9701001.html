Seat Heater System Description (If Equipped)
The seat heater system consists of a seat cushion heater (1) which includes a thermostat (4), seatback heater (2) and seat heater switch (3). The seat heater system is so designed that while the seat heater switch is in “HI” position, the seat temperature is maintained within the specified temperature by repeated ON / OFF operation of the thermostat, namely, when seat temperature is below 30 °C (86 °F) thermostat turns on the electric current and when the temperature goes up to 40 °C (104 °F), the thermostat turns off the current with the heater switch “HI” position. On the other hand, when the seat heater switch is “LO” position, the seat heater operates continuously at a low level.
