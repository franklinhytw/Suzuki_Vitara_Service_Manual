Ignition Coil Assembly Removal and Installation
Removal
1)Disconnect negative (–) cable at battery.
2)Remove air cleaner assembly and air cleaner outlet hose.  
3)Disconnect ignition coil connectors.
4)Remove ignition coil bolts (1) and then pull out ignition coil assemblies (2).
Installation
Reference: Ignition Coil Assembly Inspection:K14C
Reverse removal procedure noting the following point.
•Tighten ignition coil bolts (1) to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Ignition coil bolt (a): 11 N·m (1.1 kgf-m, 8.5 lbf-ft)
