DTC B1951
NOTE:
•As each terminal voltage is affected by the battery voltage, check that battery voltage is 12 V or more with ignition “ON”.
•Before performing, check that related fuses of headlight auto leveling control module power circuit have not blown out.
If any fuse has blown out, replace the fuse and check circuits connected to the blown fuse for short circuit to ground.
DTC Detecting Condition and Trouble Area
DTC detecting condition Trouble area
B1951: Ignition Power Supply Voltage Data Malfunction
Any of the following condition is met.
•Headlight auto leveling control module power supply circuit voltage is higher than 16.4 V for 10 sec.
•Headlight auto leveling control module power supply circuit voltage is lower than 9.3 V for 10 sec.
•Headlight auto leveling control module power supply circuit
•Headlight auto leveling control module
•Charging system

DTC check
1)Clear DTC.  
2)Set ignition “OFF”.
3)Disconnect “E18” and “E336” connectors.
4)Check for proper terminal connections to “E18” and “E336” connectors.
5)If connections are OK, connect “E18” and “E336” connectors securely.
6)Perform “DTC Confirmation Procedure” and recheck DTC.
