B-Pillar Outer Panel, Rear Door Hinge Reinforcement and Center Pillar Inner Panel Removal and Installation
NOTE:
The figure below shows condition where the following parts are removed.
•Front fender  
•Fuel filler door panel  
General Notes
•Welding method and number of welding points are the same on both sides. This repair instruction shows procedure for left side.
•Part filled with foamed material is included in parts of this repair. Therefore, repair referring to Foamed Material Filling Area Repair for precautions and repair procedure.
•The dimensions shown in the figure are reference values when cutting replacement part placed on top of existing part. Determine the most appropriate cutting (or joint) line considering both size of replacement part and condition of existing part.
Removal
1)Mark at the shown position and cut B-pillar outer panel.
NOTICE:
You may accidentally cut the parts under the B-pillar outer panel or the parts under the side sill outer panel when cutting the B-pillar outer panel.
Take sufficient care not to damage the parts under the B-pillar outer panel or the parts under the side sill outer panel when cutting the B-pillar outer panel.
2)Cut B-pillar outer panel, rear door hinge reinforcement and outer pillar inner panel at the same time at shown rough cutting position.
3)Drill out welding spots and remove B-pillar outer panel.
1: When heating around foamed material application portion to detach foamed material, be sure that temperature of said portion does not reach 170 °C (338 °F). 1. B-pillar outer panel cut position (Cut around here)
2: Grind welding spots using belt sander, if necessary.    
4)Drill out welding spots and remove upper part of rear door hinge reinforcement and upper part of center pillar inner panel.
[A]: Vehicle outside
[B]: Vehicle inside
: Grind welding spots using belt sander, if necessary.
5)Drill out welding spots and remove lower part of rear door hinge reinforcement and lower part of center pillar inner panel.
[A]: Vehicle outside [B]: Vehicle inside
Installation
NOTE:
•Before conducting any weld, check installation position and condition for installation part(s).
—Side body outside:  
—Side body inside:  
•Perform repair while taking into consideration installation of parts that affect appearance.  
1)Install lower part of rear door hinge reinforcement and lower part of center pillar inner panel.
2)Conduct resistance spot weld and plug weld.
[A]: Vehicle outside [B]: Vehicle inside
3)Install upper part of rear door hinge reinforcement and lower part of center pillar inner panel.
4)Conduct plug weld or resistance spot weld and fillet weld.
5)Install new B-pillar outer panel to vehicle body and cut in stacked condition.
6)Repair the position of B-pillar outer panel where foamed material had been filled.  
7)Install B-pillar outer panel to vehicle body.
8)Conduct plug weld or resistance spot weld and fillet weld.
9)Conduct continuous weld.
10)Apply anti-corrosion wax to back side of continuous weld part.
: Conduct resistance spot weld as three panel thickness. 1. Foamed material. 3. Rear door hinge reinforcement
“a”: 12 mm (0.47 in.) 2. B-pillar outer panel 4. Baffle plate
