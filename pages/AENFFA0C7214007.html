7B
DTC Table (Additional Heater System)
NOTE:
When ECM detects DTCs B1536 and/or B1537, both current and history DTCs are displayed at the same time.
DTC DTC name DTC detecting condition
B1536   Additional Heater Relay No.1 Output Malfunction Output voltage of additional heater relay No.1 is lower than specified value continuously.
B1537   Additional Heater Relay No.2 and No.3 Output Malfunction Output voltage of additional heater relay No.2 and/or No.3 is lower than specified value continuously.
