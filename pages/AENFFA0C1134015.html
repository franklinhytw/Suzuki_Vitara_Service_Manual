DTC Detecting Condition and Trouble Area
DTC detection condition Trouble area
P0036: HO2S Heater Control Circuit (Sensor2)
A/F sensor-2 heater drive circuit is open.
(3 D/C detecting logic)
•A/F sensor-2 heater and/or its circuit
•ECM
P0037: HO2S Heater Control Circuit Low (Sensor2)
A/F sensor-2 heater drive circuit is shorted to ground.
(3 D/C detecting logic)
P0038: HO2S Heater Control Circuit High (Sensor2)
A/F sensor-2 heater drive circuit is shorted to power supply.
(3 D/C detecting logic)
P0054: HO2S Heater Resistance (Sensor2)
Calculated A/F sensor-2 temperature is out of specified range.
(3 D/C detecting logic)
