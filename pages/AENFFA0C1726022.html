Fuel Level Sensor Inspection
Reference: Fuel Pump Assembly Removal and Installation:K14C
•Check that resistance between terminals “a” and “b” of fuel level sensor changes depending on float position.
•Check resistance between terminals “a” and “b” at each float position in the following table.
If check result is not as specified, replace fuel pump assembly.
Fuel level sensor specifications (market code P37)
Float Position Resistance (Ω)
“c” 17.1 – 21.1 mm (0.673 – 0.830 in.) 280 ± 2.8
“d” 113.2 – 117.2 mm (4.457 – 4.614 in.) 160 ± 2
“e” 180.4 – 184.4 mm (7.102 – 7.259 in.) 40 ± 1.5
