Oil Temperature Sensor Removal and Installation
Reference: Oil Temperature Sensor On-Vehicle Inspection:K14C
Removal
1)Disconnect negative (–) cable at battery.
2)Disconnect oil temperature sensor connector (1).
3)Remove oil temperature sensor (2) from cylinder block.
Installation
Reverse removal procedure noting the following point.
•Use new gasket.
•Tighten oil temperature sensor (1) to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Oil temperature sensor (a): 25 N·m (2.5 kgf-m, 18.5 lbf-ft)
