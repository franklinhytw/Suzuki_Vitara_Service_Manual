Propeller Shaft Inspection (4WD Model)
1)Check propeller shaft connecting bolts for looseness. If loose bolt is found, tighten it to specified torque.
2)Check propeller shaft joints for wear, play and damage. If any defect is found, replace joint(s).
3)Check propeller shaft center support for foreign material trapped in it, abnormal noise, crack and other damage. If any defect is found, replace center support.
