Precautions for Sun Roof
Water Leakage Test
•Perform water leakage test after installing sun roof glasses.
•Adjust sun roof glass mount position again in case of water leakage. (Replace if sun roof weather-strips included in sun roof glasses are defective.)
•Do not apply excessive water pressure during water leakage test.
Installation and Replacement
•Do not apply large impact to sun roof motor, such as by dropping it.
•Do not pinch any object during initialization of sun roof motor, sun roof glass cable and sunshade cable.
Anti-Pinching Function Check
•Do not use any part of your body such as hand or arm to check this function. Pay enough attention to avoid body from being pinched during check.
•Anti-pinching function works in auto sliding function in closing direction and auto tilt-down function. Do not check in manual operation function.
•Roof may deform if hard material such as hammer handle is pinched. (Use hammer handle wrapped with cloth.)
