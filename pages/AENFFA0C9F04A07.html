Rearview image does not come out even when select lever is shifted to “R” range (if equipped)
Before performing the following troubleshooting, check if the symptom is not caused by incorrect handling while referring to Owner’s Manual.
If the cause cannot be identified by the following troubleshooting, replace audio unit with a known-good one. If the symptom is cleared, audio unit is the cause.
Condition Possible Cause Action
Rearview image does not come out even when select lever is shifted to “R” range (if equipped)
Rearview image signal from rearview camera inputted incorrectly
Check rearview camera circuit.  
“Reverse” position signal inputted incorrectly
Check “Reverse” position signal circuit.
