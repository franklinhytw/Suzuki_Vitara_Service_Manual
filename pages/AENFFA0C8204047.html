B1071-1A: Left-Side Rear Seat Belt Pretensioner Circuit Low Resistance
Left-side rear seat belt pretensioner circuit resistance is less than specified range.
•Left-side and/or right-side rear seat belt pretensioner circuit
•Left-side and/or right-side rear seat belt pretensioner
•SDM
B1072-1A: Right-Side Rear Seat Belt Pretensioner Circuit Low Resistance
Right-side rear seat belt pretensioner circuit resistance is less than specified range.

DTC Troubleshooting
Step Action Yes No
1
Was “Air Bag Diagnostic System Check” performed?
Go to Step 2.
Go to Air Bag Diagnostic System Check.
2
Seat belt pretensioner check
1)Check that ignition is “OFF”.
2)Disconnect “L275” or “L276” connector.  
3)Connect “STEERING WHEEL” harness (1) of special tool (C), special tools (A) and (B) to “L275” or “L276” connector.
Special Tool
(A): 09932–76010
(B): 09932–78310
(C): 09932–75011
4)Set ignition “ON”.
5)Check for DTC.  
Is DTC B1071-1A or DTC B1072-1A indicated?
Go to Step 3.
Replace rear seat belt pretensioner.  
3
Floor harness circuit check
1)Set ignition “OFF”.
2)Disconnect “L05” connector.  
3)Release shorting bar in SDM connector inserting release tool (B).
4)Check the following points.
•Resistance between “L05-28” and “L05-27” terminals (LHD model: DTC B1071-1A, RHD model: DTC B1072-1A): infinity
•Resistance between “L05-29” and “L05-30” terminals (LHD model: DTC B1072-1A, RHD model: DTC B1071-1A): infinity
Special Tool
(A): 09932–76010
(B): 09932–75100
Is check result OK?
Replace SDM and recheck DTC.  
Replace floor harness.
NOTE:
Upon completion of inspection and repair work, perform the following items.
•Reconnect all air bag system components and check that all components are properly mounted.
•Clear DTCs, referring to DTC Clearance if any DTC is detected.
•Repeat Air Bag Diagnostic System Check to check that the trouble has been solved.
