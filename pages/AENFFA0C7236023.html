A/C Refrigerant Pressure Sensor and Its Circuit Inspection
Reference: A/C Refrigerant Pressure Sensor Removal and Installation:Automatic Type
1)Disconnect A/C refrigerant pressure sensor connector.
2)Set ignition “ON”.
3)Check that voltage between “GRY” wire terminal and “LT GRN” wire terminal of A/C refrigerant pressure sensor connector is 4.75 V to 5.25 V.
If check result is not as specified, check A/C refrigerant pressure sensor circuit.
4)Connect A/C refrigerant pressure sensor connector with ignition “OFF”.
5)Connect manifold gauge set to charging valves.
6)Check A/C refrigerant pressure sensor voltage of ECM connector.
