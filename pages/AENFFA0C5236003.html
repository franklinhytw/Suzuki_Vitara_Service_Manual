5B
Differential Side Oil Seal Replacement
1)Hoist vehicle and drain transaxle oil.  
2)Remove front drive shaft assembly and drive intermediate shaft assembly.
•Front drive shaft assembly:  
•Drive intermediate shaft assembly:  
3)Remove oil seal (1) using special tool.
Special Tool
(A): 09913–50121
4)Install a new oil seal (1) to specified position using special tool.
NOTE:
•When installing oil seal, face its spring side inward.
•Check that oil seal is installed horizontally to surface of case after installing oil seal.
Special Tool
(A): 09926–27610
(B): 09944–66010
(C): 09924–74510
Distance from case end face to left oil seal for 2WD / 4WD
“a”: 3.0 – 3.6 mm (0.12 – 0.14 in.)
Distance from case end face to right oil seal for 2WD
“b”: 10 – 10.6 mm (0.40 – 0.41 in.)
5)Apply grease to oil seal lip. Then, check drive shaft where oil seal contacts and check its smoothness.
“A”: Grease 99000–25011 (SUZUKI Super Grease A)
6)Insert front drive shaft assembly and drive intermediate shaft assembly.

•Drive intermediate shaft assembly:
