Sunshade Removal and Installation
Reference: Sun Roof Glass Removal and Installation
Reference: Sun Roof Assembly Removal and Installation
Removal
1)Remove sun roof motor (for sunshade).  
2)Remove 2 screws and then slide sunshade (1) in arrow direction.
3)Remove sunshade bases (2), sunshade stay (3) and sunshade cables from sunshade.  
Installation
Reverse removal procedure noting the following points.
•Use new screws.
•Check sunshade cables and sun roof motor gear (for sunshade) for wear and damage.
•Apply grease to sliding parts.
