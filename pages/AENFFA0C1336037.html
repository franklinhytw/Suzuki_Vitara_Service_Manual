CKP Sensor Inspection
Reference: CKP Sensor Removal and Installation:D16AA
1)Check CKP sensor and sensor rotor teeth are free from any metal particles and damage.
2)Check resistance between terminals of CKP sensor is within specified range.
If resistance is not as specified, replace CKP sensor.
CKP sensor resistance
600 to 1,000 Ω at 20 °C (68 °F)
