Intermittent wiper relay circuit
1)Turn the windshield wiper switch to “INT” position.
2)Connect battery positive (+) terminal to terminal “8” and its negative (–) terminal to terminal “5”.
3)Connect voltmeter positive lead to terminal “7” and its negative lead to terminal “5”.
4)Check that the voltmeter indicates the battery voltage (10 – 14 V).
5)Connect terminal “6” and terminal “8” by a jumper wire.
[A]: RHD with rear fog light and LHD [B]: RHD without rear fog light
6)Disconnect end of the jumper wire from terminal “8”.
7)Connect disconnected jumper wire end to terminal “5”, and then check that voltage between terminal “7” and terminal “5” changes as shown.
If check result is not as specified, replace wiper and washer switch.

3)Connect voltmeter positive lead to terminal “7” and its negative lead to terminal “5”.
