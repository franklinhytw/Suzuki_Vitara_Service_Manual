1B
EGR Valve Inspection
EGR-1 Valve
Visual check
Check EGR-1 valve for damage on valve and valve gasket. If faulty condition is found, replace EGR-1 valve.  
Operation check
1)When ignition is “OFF”, connect SUZUKI scan tool to DLC.
2)Set ignition “ON”, and select “EGR-1 test” under “Utility” mode on SUZUKI scan tool.
3)Check operating sound from EGR-1 valve. If EGR-1 valve does not operate. Replace EGR-1 valve.
