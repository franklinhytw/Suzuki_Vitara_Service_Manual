P0401: Exhaust Gas Recirculation “A” Flow Insufficient Detected
Measured EGR flow is lower than specified value varied depending on engine speed.
(3 D/C detection logic)
•MAF sensor and/or its circuit
•EGR-1 valve and/or its circuit
•EGR-1 valve position sensor and/or its circuit
•EGR-2 valve and/or its circuit
•EGR-2 valve position sensor and/or its circuit
•Inlet throttle valve and/or its circuit
•Air intake circuit leakage or clog
•ECM
P0402: Exhaust Gas Recirculation “A” Flow Excessive Detected
Measured EGR flow is higher than specified value varied depending on engine speed.
(1 D/C detection logic)
