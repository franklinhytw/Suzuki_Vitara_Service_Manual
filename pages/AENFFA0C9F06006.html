Steering Switch (Audio Control Switch and Hands-Free Switch) Removal and Installation (If Equipped)
Removal
1)Remove steering wheel referring to Steering Wheel Removal and Installation.
2)Remove screws and disconnect connector (1).
3)Remove steering wheel cover (2) and hands-free switch (3) (if equipped) from steering wheel.
4)Remove audio control switch (4) and cruise control switch (5) (if equipped) from steering wheel.
Installation
Reference: Steering Switch (Audio Control Switch and Hands-Free Switch) Inspection (If Equipped)
Reverse removal procedure.
