Radiator Cooling Fan Disassembly and Reassembly
Reference: Radiator Cooling Fan Assembly Removal and Installation:D16AA
Disassembly
1)Remove radiator cooling fan (1) from radiator cooling fan motor (2) and radiator cooling sub fan motor (3).
2)Remove radiator cooling fan motor and radiator cooling sub fan motor from radiator cooling fan shroud (4).
Reassembly
Reverse disassembly procedure noting the following point.
•Tighten each bolt and nut to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Radiator cooling fan nut (a): 6.3 N·m (0.64 kgf-m, 5.0 lbf-ft)
Radiator cooling fan bolt (b): 3.9 N·m (0.40 kgf-m, 3.0 lbf-ft)
Radiator cooling fan motor bolt (C): 3.9 N·m (0.40 kgf-m, 3.0 lbf-ft)
