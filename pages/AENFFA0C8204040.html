B106C-1B: Curtain Air Bag Circuit High Resistance (Driver Side)
Driver-side curtain air bag (inflator) module circuit resistance is more than specified range.
•Driver-side and/or passenger-side curtain air bag (inflator) module circuit
•Driver-side and/or passenger-side curtain air bag (inflator) module
•SDM
B106D-1B: Curtain Air Bag Circuit High Resistance (Passenger Side)
Passenger-side curtain air bag (inflator) module circuit resistance is more than specified range.

DTC Troubleshooting
Step Action Yes No
1
Was “Air Bag Diagnostic System Check” performed?
Go to Step 2.
Go to Air Bag Diagnostic System Check.
2
Curtain air bag (inflator) module Check
1)Check that ignition is “OFF”.
2)Check for proper connection to “L290” or “L291” connector.
3)Disconnect “L290” or “L291” connector.  
4)Connect “STEERING WHEEL” harness (1) of special tool (C), special tools (A) and (B) to “L290” or “L291” connector.
Special Tool
(A): 09932–76010
(B): 09932–78310
(C): 09932–75011
5)Set ignition “ON”.
6)Check for DTC.  
Is DTC B106C-1B or DTC B106D-1B indicated?
Go to Step 3.
Replace curtain air bag (inflator) module.  
3
Floor harness circuit check
1)Check for proper connection to “L05” connector.
2)Disconnect “L05” connector.  
3)Release shorting bar in SDM connector inserting release tool (B).
4)Check the following points.
•Resistance of “A1” and “A2” circuits (DTC B106C-1B): 1 Ω or less
•Resistance of “A3” and “A4” circuits (DTC B106D-1B): 1 Ω or less
Special Tool
(A): 09932–76010
(B): 09932–75100
Is check result OK?
Replace SDM and recheck DTC.  
Replace floor harness.
NOTE:
Upon completion of inspection and repair work, perform the following items.
•Reconnect all air bag system components and check that all components are properly mounted.
•Clear DTCs, referring to DTC Clearance if any DTC is detected.
•Repeat Air Bag Diagnostic System Check to check that the trouble has been solved.
