1G
Fuel Line and Connection Inspection
WARNING:
Fuel in the fuel feed line (1) is under high pressure and can spray out causing a fire and/or personal injury.
Use special care when checking the fuel feed line.
Before performing the following procedure, observe Precautions for Fuel System Service:D16AA.
Visually check fuel lines and connections for evidence of fuel leakage, crack and deterioration of hose or other damage.
Check that all clamps are secure.
Replace hoses that shows sign of crack.
