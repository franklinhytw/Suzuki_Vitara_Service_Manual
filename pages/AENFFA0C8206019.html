8B
Side-Air Bag (Inflator) Module Inspection (8, 9 or 11-Channel Air Bag System)
Reference: Side-Air Bag (Inflator) Module Removal and Installation (8, 9 or 11-Channel Air Bag System)
WARNING:
Failure to take proper precautions when checking the side-air bag (inflator) module can result in personal injury due to accidental deployment of the air bag.
Never measure the resistance of the air bag (inflator) module.
Check air bag (inflator) module and if any of the following conditions is found, replace air bag (inflator) module with new one.
•Air bag being deployed.
•Air bag (inflator) module is damaged or a strong impact was applied to it.
•Connector being damaged.
•Bend or deformation of air bag (inflator) module bracket.
