Brake Booster Removal and Installation
Reference: Brake Booster Components
NOTICE:
The brake master cylinder and brake booster will lose their original performance if they are disassembled and reassembled.
If any faulty condition is found in the brake master cylinder or brake booster, replace the part rather than trying to disassemble and repair it.

4)For ENG A-STOP model, disconnect brake booster pressure sensor connector.

7)Remove clip (1) and clevis pin (2).
8)Remove brake booster nuts (1) and remove brake booster from dash panel.
9)Remove vacuum check valve (1) or brake booster pressure sensor (2) from brake booster (3).

Installation
Reference: Brake Booster Inspection
LHD
1)Install vacuum check valve (1) or brake booster pressure sensor (2) to brake booster (3).
[A]: Non-ENG A-STOP model [B]: ENG A-STOP model
2)Remove combination meter.  
3)Loosen brake pedal bracket nut (1).
4)Install brake booster to dash panel with new gasket. Then connect clevis (3) to brake pedal arm with clevis pin (2) and new clip (1).
5)Tighten brake booster nuts to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Brake booster nut (a): 13 N·m (1.3 kgf-m, 9.5 lbf-ft)
6)Tighten brake pedal bracket nut to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Brake pedal bracket nut (a): 13 N·m (1.3 kgf-m, 9.5 lbf-ft)
7)Install instrument panel undercover.
8)Install combination meter.  
9)Connect vacuum hose to brake booster.
10)For ENG A-STOP model, connect brake booster pressure sensor connector.
11)Install brake master cylinder assembly to brake booster.  
12)Bleed brake system.  
13)Check installed parts for brake fluid leakage.
14)Check brake pedal for play.  
15)Check that brake system operates properly without brake fluid leakage.
16)For D16AA model, install the following parts.
•Air cleaner assembly and air cleaner bracket:  
•Battery:  
17)For K14C model, install the following parts.
•Battery:  
•Air cleaner assembly:  
•Intercooler outlet hose No.2.
