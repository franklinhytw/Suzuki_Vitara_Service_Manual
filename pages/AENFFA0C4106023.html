4A
Brake Booster Inspection
Reference: Brake Booster Removal and Installation
•Check brake booster for damage.
•Check brake booster for operation referring to “Operation Check” under Brake Booster Function Check.
•Check boot for damage and deterioration.
•Check push rod length.
If check result is not as specified, replace brake booster.
Push rod length
“a”: 114.5 – 115.5 mm (4.507 – 4.547 in.)
