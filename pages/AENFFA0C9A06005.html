Front Fender Removal and Installation
Reference: Front Fender Components
Removal
1)Remove front bumper and front bumper holder.  
2)Remove cowl top garnish.  
3)Remove headlight unit.  
4)Remove side turn signal light (if equipped).  
5)Remove side sill guard.  
6)Remove front fender garnish if necessary.
7)Remove front fender lining.
8)Remove front fender and front fender rear pad.
Installation
Reverse removal procedure noting the following points.
•Adjust front fender panel clearance.  
•If paint on front fender bolts is peeled off, apply paint again to prevent rust.
•Perform headlight aiming after installation.
