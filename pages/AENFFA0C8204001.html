8B
Air Bag Diagnostic System Check
Referring to Precautions for Air Bag System Service, check air bag system according to the following procedure.
Step Action Yes No
1
DTC check
1)Check DTC referring to DTC Check.
NOTE:
If communication between SUZUKI scan tool and vehicle cannot be established, perform “DLC and Its Communication Check (No Communication to SDM)”.
2)Clear DTC referring to DTC Clearance.
Is there any DTC(s) when rechecking?
Go to applicable DTC diagnosis flow, and go to Step 5.
Go to Step 2.
2
Visual inspection
1)Perform visual inspection referring to Visual Inspection.
Is there any faulty condition?
Repair faulty part or replace it, and go to Step 5.
Go to Step 3.
3
Impact sign check
1)Check vehicle for evidence of impact.
Are there any signs of impact?
Check faulty connections and replace them according to Repair and Inspection Required after Accident, and go to Step 5.
Go to Step 4.
4
Intermittent problem check
1)Check intermittent problems referring to Intermittent Connection and Poor Contact Inspection.
Is there any faulty condition?
Repair faulty part or replace it, and go to Step 5.
Go to Step 5.
5
Final confirmation test
1)Check that problematic symptom has gone and air bag system is free from any abnormal conditions. If what has been repaired is related to DTC, clear DTC once and check that no DTC is indicated.
Is malfunction detected?
Return to Step 1.
End.
