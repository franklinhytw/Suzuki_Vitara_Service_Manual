9D
Washer Tank and Washer Pump Removal and Installation
Removal
1)Remove front bumper.  
2)Disconnect wire harness clamps, washer pump connectors and washer hoses.
3)Remove washer tank clip (1) and bolts (2), and remove washer tank (3).
4)Remove windshield washer pump (1), rear washer pump (2) from washer tank.
Installation
Reference: Washer Pump Inspection
Reverse removal procedure noting the following point.
•Tighten washer tank bolts in numerical order (“1” – “3”) to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Washer tank bolt (a): 3.9 N·m (0.40 kgf-m, 3.0 lbf-ft)
