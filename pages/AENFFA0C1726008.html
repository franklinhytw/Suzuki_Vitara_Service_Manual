High Pressure Pipe Removal and Installation
Before performing the following procedure, observe Precautions for Fuel System Service:K14C.
Removal
1)Release fuel pressure.  
2)Remove air cleaner assembly.  
3)Remove electric throttle body assembly.  
4)Remove high pressure fuel pump cover.
5)Remove high pressure pipe.
Installation
NOTICE:
Failure to take proper precautions when installing the high pressure pipe can cause damage to the high pressure pipe and may lead to fuel leakage.
Take the following precautions when removing and installing the high pressure pipe.
•Never reuse high pressure pipe.
•When the union nut is tightened using extension (claw foot) until the torque wrench reads the specified torque, the nut will actually be tightened to a torque much more than the specified torque. This could damage the thread.
Use the formula shown below to determine the correct reading of the torque wrench for its length. Then tighten the union nut of high pressure pipe until the torque wrench indicates the value thus determined.
Tightening torque formula.
M = T x L / (L + “a”)
M: Value indicated on torque wrench
T: Specified tightening torque
L: Effective length of torque wrench
“a”: Effective length of extension
“b”: Handle center line (power point) “c”: Pivot point (fulcrum)
1)Install new high pressure pipe (1) according to the following procedure.
a)Check that notch (1) on crank pulley is positioned within range “b”.
“a”: 10° “b”: 20°
b)Loosen high pressure fuel pump bolts (1), tighten them to 5 N·m (0.5 kgf-m, 4.0 lbf-ft) and then loosen 90 – 120°.
c)Tighten high pressure pipe union nuts (2) by hand until seated and temporarily tighten high pressure pipe bolt (3).
d)Tighten high pressure pipe union nuts (1) to specified torque in numerical order (“1” – “2”) as shown in figure.
Tightening torque
High pressure pipe union nut (a): 33 N·m (3.4 kgf-m, 24.5 lbf-ft)
e)Tighten high pressure fuel pump bolts (2) to specified torque.
Tightening torque
High pressure fuel pump bolt (b): 13 N·m (1.3 kgf-m, 9.5 lbf-ft)
f)Tighten high pressure pipe bolt (3).
2)Install high pressure fuel pump cover.
3)Install electric throttle body assembly.  
4)Install air cleaner assembly.  
5)Check for fuel leakage.
