5F
Hydraulic Circuit Depressurization Procedure
1)Set ignition “OFF”.
2)Connect SUZUKI scan tool to DLC (1).
Special Tool
(A): SUZUKI scan tool (Suzuki SDT-II)
3)Set ignition “ON”.
4)Select “Hydraulic Circuit Depressurization” function under “Utility” mode on SUZUKI scan tool, and follow the instructions displayed on SUZUKI scan tool.
5)After completing “Hydraulic Circuit Depressurization”, set ignition “OFF” and disconnect SUZUKI scan tool from DLC.
