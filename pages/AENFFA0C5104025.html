P0711: Transmission Fluid Temperature Sensor “A” Circuit Range / Performance
ATF temperature sensor signal circuit voltage is unchanged for 10 minutes during driving.
(2 D/C detection logic)
•Solenoid harness with ATF temperature sensor
•TCM
P0712: Transmission Fluid Temperature Sensor “A” Circuit Low
ATF temperature sensor signal circuit voltage is lower than specified value for 5 sec.
(2 D/C detection logic)
P0713: Transmission Fluid Temperature Sensor “A” Circuit High
ATF temperature sensor signal circuit voltage is higher than specified value for 5 sec. after driving.
(2 D/C detection logic)
DTC Confirmation Procedure
P0711 / P0713
WARNING:
If you do not select a suitable route and take proper precautions when performing a road test, an accident can occur.
•Select a level road with no traffic to minimize the risk of accident.
•Perform the test extremely carefully.
•Perform the road test using 2 persons, a driver and a tester.
1)Warm up engine to normal operating temperature.
2)Drive vehicle for 10 minutes or more.
P0712
1)Set ignition “ON” for 10 sec.
