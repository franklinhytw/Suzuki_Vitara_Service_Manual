Thermostat Assembly Removal and Installation
Removal
1)Remove battery and battery tray.  
2)Drain coolant.  
3)Disconnect water hoses (1) from thermostat assembly (2).
4)Remove thermostat assembly from EGR-1 valve mounting.
Installation
Reverse removal procedure noting the following points.
•Use new O-ring.
•Tighten thermostat bolts to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Thermostat bolt: 8.0 N·m (0.82 kgf-m, 6.0 lbf-ft)
•Refill cooling system with coolant.  
•Check coolant system for leakage.
