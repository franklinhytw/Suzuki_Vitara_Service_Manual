Remounting
Reverse dismounting procedure noting the following points.
•Apply grease to new O-ring (1), and install it to transfer left case (2).
•Apply 3 g (0.10 oz) of grease to boss outer surface of transfer left case shown in figure.
“A”: Grease 99000–25011 (SUZUKI Super Grease A)
•Tighten transfer stiffener bolts (1) in numerical order as shown in figure.
a.Temporarily tighten transfer stiffener bolts in numerical order (“1” – “2”) temporarily.
b.Press transfer stiffener (2) against transfer and tighten transfer stiffener bolts to specified torque in numerical order (“3” – “6”).
Tightening torque
Transfer stiffener bolt* (a): 55 N·m (5.6 kgf-m, 40.5 lbf-ft)
c.Recheck that transfer stiffener bolts are tightened in numerical order (“7” – “10”).
[A]: Petrol model [B]: Diesel model
•Tighten transfer bolts and nut(s) to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Transfer bolt: 88 N·m (9.0 kgf-m, 65.0 lbf-ft)
Transfer nut: 88 N·m (9.0 kgf-m, 65.0 lbf-ft)
