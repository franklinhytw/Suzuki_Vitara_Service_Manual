6C
Steering Force Check
1)Place vehicle on level road and set steering wheel in straight ahead position.
2)Check that tire inflation pressure is as specified referring to the “Tire Placard”.
3)Start engine.
4)With engine idling, measure steering force using spring balance (1).
Steering force
: 18 – 25 N (1.8 – 2.5 kgf, 4.0 – 5.6 lbf)
