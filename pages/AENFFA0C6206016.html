Ignition Switch Inspection (Ignition Switch Model)
Reference: Ignition Switch Removal and Installation (Ignition Switch Model)
1)Remove steering column lower cover.  
2)Disconnect ignition switch connector from ignition switch.
Check for continuity between terminals at each switch position. If check result is not as specified, replace ignition switch.
Ignition switch continuity
3)Connect connector to ignition switch.
4)Install steering column lower cover.
