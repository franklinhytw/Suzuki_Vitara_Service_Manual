DTC P2687 / P2688 / P2689
DTC Detecting Condition and Trouble Area
DTC detecting condition Trouble area
P2687: Fuel Supply Heater Control Circuit / Open
Fuel heater relay drive circuit is open.
(1 D/C detection logic)
•Fuel heater relay and/or its circuit
•ECM
P2688: Fuel Supply Heater Control Circuit Low
Fuel heater relay drive circuit is shorted to ground.
(1 D/C detection logic)
P2689: Fuel Supply Heater Control Circuit High
Any of the following conditions is met.
•Fuel heater relay drive circuit is shorted to power supply.
•Temperature of fuel heater relay drive circuit is out of specified range.
(1 D/C detection logic)
