Manual Transaxle Assembly Disassembly and Reassembly
Reference: Manual Transaxle Unit Dismounting and Remounting:D16AA-6M/T Model
Reference: Manual Transaxle Assembly Components:D16AA-6M/T Model
Reference: Gear Shift and Select Shaft Assembly Removal and Installation:D16AA-6M/T Model
Disassembly
1)Remove gear shift locating bolt (1), and then take out locating spring and steel ball.
2)Remove transaxle case bolts.
3)Remove hole cap (1) using flat-bladed screwdriver (2) or the like.
4)Open countershaft circlip (2) and remove transaxle left side case (1) from right case using special tool while opening circlip.
Special Tool
(A): 09912–34510
3. Countershaft left bearing
5)Remove reverse gear shift lever bolts (1) and reverse gear shift lever (2).
6)Tapping input shaft end with plastic hammer, push it out as assembly from case a little, and then take out input shaft assembly (1), countershaft assembly (2), high speed gear shift shaft (3), low speed gear shift shaft (4), 5th and 6th gear shift shaft (5), reverse gear shift (6) and reverse gears (7).
7)Remove countershaft circlip from left case, if necessary.
8)Remove differential assembly from right case.
Reassembly
1)Install differential assembly into right case.
2)Join input shaft (5), countershaft (4), low speed gear shift shaft (2), high speed gear shift shaft (3), 5th and 6th gear shift shaft (6), reverse gear shift shaft (7) and reverse gears (8) assemblies all together, and then install them into right case (1) so as not to damage oil seal by input shaft spline.
NOTE:
•Input shaft right bearing on shaft can be installed into right case (1) by tapping shaft with plastic hammer.
•While installing shafts and gears, check the following points.
—Countershaft is engaged with final gear.
—Reverse gear shaft pin (9) is engaged with cutout (10).
3)Select input shaft shim and reverse gear washer as follows.
a)Measure depth “a” of left case (1) and “b” of right case (2) and measure depth “e” of left case and “f” of right case.
b)Measure distance “c” of input shaft assembly (3) and measure distance “g” of reverse gear assembly (4).
c)Calculate the clearance of “d” and “h” by the following formulas.
Clearance “d” = Depth “a” + Depth “b” – Distance “c”
Clearance “h” = Depth “e” + Depth “f” – Distance “g”
d)Select shims closest to clearance “d” and select washer closest to clearance “h” from the following available sizes.

4)Tighten reverse gear shift lever new bolts (1).
Tightening torque
Reverse gear shift lever bolt (a): 25 N·m (2.5 kgf-m, 18.5 lbf-ft)
5)Clean mating surfaces of both right and left cases, apply sealant to right case (1) as shown in figure by such amount that its section is 1.5 mm (0.059 in.) in diameter and mate it with left case (2).
“A”: Sealant 99000–31260 (SUZUKI Bond No.1217G)
6)Tighten case bolts (3) to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Transaxle case bolt (a): 55 N·m (5.6 kgf-m, 40.5 lbf-ft)
7)Install steel ball (1) and locating spring (2) and tighten new bolt (3).
Tightening torque
Gear shift locating bolt (a): 15 N·m (1.5 kgf-m, 11.0 lbf-ft)
8)Check that new circlip (1) is installed to countershaft left side bearing (2) and then press-fit new hole cap (3) to left case (5).
