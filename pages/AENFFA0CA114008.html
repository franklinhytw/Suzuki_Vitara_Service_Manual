P0575: Cruise Control Input Circuit
This DTC is detected if any one of the following conditions is met.
•Signal voltage of cruise control switch signal circuit is higher than specified voltage for specified time continuously.
•ON signals of any or all of following switches of cruise control system are detected for longer than specified time.
—CRUISE (main) switch
—LIMIT switch
—CANCEL switch
—RES/+ switch
—SET/– switch
•Cruise control switch circuit / connector

DTC Troubleshooting
Step Action Yes No
1
Parameter check
1)Connect SUZUKI scan tool to DLC when ignition is “OFF”.
2)Set ignition “ON”.
3)Check “Cruise CANCEL Switch”, “Cruise RES/ACC Switch”, “Cruise SET/COAST Switch” and “Cruise ON/OFF Switch” displayed on SUZUKI scan tool.  
Is check result OK?
Check for intermittent trouble.  
Go to Step 2.
2
Cruise control switch circuit check
1)Check that ignition is “OFF”.
2)Disconnect “E01” and “G371” connectors.
3)Check for proper terminal connection to “E01” and “G371” connectors.
4)If connections are OK, check the following points.
•Resistance of “A1” and “A2” circuits: less than 1 Ω
•Resistance between “A1” circuit and ground: infinity
•Resistance between “A1” circuit terminal and other terminals at “G371” connector: infinity
•Resistance between “A2” circuit terminal and other terminals at “G371” connector: infinity
•Voltage of “A1” and “A2” circuits: approx. 0 V (When ignition is “ON”)
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 3.
Repair or replace defective wire harness.
3
Contact coil circuit check
1)Disconnect “S62” connector.
2)Check the following points.
•Resistance between “S62-5” terminal and “S173-5” terminal of contact coil connector: less than 1 Ω
•Resistance between “S62-6” terminal and “S173-6” terminal of contact coil connector: less than 1 Ω
•Resistance between “5” terminal and other terminal at “S173” connector: infinity
•Resistance between “6” terminal and other terminal at “S173” connector: infinity
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 4.
Replace contact coil.  
4
Cruise control switch check
1)Check cruise control switch.  
Is check result OK?
Replace ECM and recheck DTC.
