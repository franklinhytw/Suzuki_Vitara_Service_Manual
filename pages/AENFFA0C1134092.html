DTC Detecting Condition and Trouble Area
DTC detecting condition Trouble area
P2453: Diesel Particulate Filter Pressure Sensor “A” Circuit Range / Performance
Any of the following conditions is met.
•DPF® differential pressure is lower then specified value.
•Difference between pressure of upstream of DPF® and barometric pressure is higher than specified value.
(1 D/C detection logic)
•DPF® differential pressure sensor and/or its circuit
•DPF® differential pressure sensor pipe and/or hose
•DPF®
•ECM
P2454: Diesel Particulate Filter Pressure Sensor “A” Circuit Low
Output voltage of DPF® differential pressure sensor signal circuit is lower than specified value.
(1 D/C detection logic)
P2455: Diesel Particulate Filter Pressure Sensor “A” Circuit High
Output voltage of DPF® differential pressure sensor signal circuit is higher than specified value.
(1 D/C detection logic)
