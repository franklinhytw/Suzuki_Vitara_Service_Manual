EGT Sensor Removal and Installation
EGT Sensor-1
Removal
1)Disconnect negative (–) cable at battery.
2)Remove engine cover.  
3)Disconnect connector from EGT sensor-1 (1) as follows.
a)Move connector lock pin (2) in arrow direction as shown in figure.
b)Push connector lock pin and disconnect connector (3) from EGT sensor-1.
4)Remove EGT sensor-1 from DPF®.
Installation
Reference: EGT Sensor Inspection:D16AA
Reverse removal procedure noting the following points.
•Install EGT sensor-1 (1) to DPF®. Align protrusion (2) of EGT sensor-1 with groove (3) of DPF®.
•Tighten EGT sensor-1 to specified torque.
Tightening torque
EGT sensor-1 (a): 45 N·m (4.6 kgf-m, 33.5 lbf-ft)
