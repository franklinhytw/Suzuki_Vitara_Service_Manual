DTC Detecting Condition and Trouble Area
DTC detecting condition Trouble area
B1090-14: Driver Forward Impact sensor Circuit Shorted to Ground
Driver-side forward impact sensor circuit has been shorted to ground.
•Driver-side and/or passenger-side forward impact sensor
•Driver-side and/or passenger-side forward impact sensor circuit
•SDM
B1090-15: Driver Forward Impact Sensor Circuit Shorted to Power Supply
Driver-side forward impact sensor circuit has been shorted to power supply or opened.
B1090-87: Driver Forward Impact sensor Communication Data Invalid
Driver-side forward impact sensor has communicated with invalid data.
B1091-14: Passenger Forward Impact Sensor Circuit Shorted to Ground
Passenger-side forward impact sensor circuit has been shorted to ground.
B1091-15: Passenger Forward Impact Sensor Circuit Shorted to Power Supply
Passenger-side forward impact sensor circuit has been shorted to power supply or opened.
B1091-87: Passenger Forward Impact sensor Communication Data Invalid
Passenger-side forward impact sensor has communicated with invalid data.

NOTE:
Upon completion of inspection and repair work, perform the following items.

•Clear DTCs, referring to DTC Clearance if any DTC is detected.
