3B
Rear Differential Oil Change
1)Hoist vehicle and check oil leakage.
If leakage exists, correct it.
2)Remove rear differential oil level / filler plug (2).
3)Remove rear differential oil drain plug (1), and drain oil.
4)Apply sealant to thread of rear differential drain plug, and tighten it to specified torque.
“A”: Sealant 99000–31260 (SUZUKI Bond No.1217G)
Tightening torque
Rear differential oil drain plug (a): 23 N·m (2.3 kgf-m, 17.0 lbf-ft)
5)Replenish rear differential with new specified oil up to rear differential oil level / filler plug hole (3).
NOTE:
•Hypoid gear oil must be used for rear differential.
•It is highly recommended to use “SUZUKI GEAR OIL 75W-85”.
Rear differential oil specification
SUZUKI GEAR OIL 99000-22B30
API GL-5 (For SAE classification, refer to viscosity chart.)
Viscosity chart
[a]: Outside air temperature
Rear differential oil capacity
Reference: 0.7 – 0.9 liters (1.48/1.23 – 1.90/1.58 US/Imp. pt.)
6)Apply sealant to thread of rear differential oil level / filler plug, and then tighten it to specified torque.
“A”: Sealant 99000–31260 (SUZUKI Bond No.1217G)
Tightening torque
Rear differential oil level / filler plug (b): 23 N·m (2.3 kgf-m, 17.0 lbf-ft)
