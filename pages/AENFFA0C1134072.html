DTC P1541
DTC Detecting Condition and Trouble Area
DTC detecting condition Trouble area
P1541: VDOP Solenoid Valve / Oil Pressure Switch 2 Voltage Correlation
Any of the following conditions is met.
•Oil pressure switch-2 signal voltage is at high level even when VDOP solenoid valve is not opened with engine running.
•High oil pressure is not recognized with engine running.
(1 D/C detection logic or 1 D/C detection logic but MIL does not light up)
•VDOP solenoid valve and/or its circuit
•Oil pressure switch-2 and/or its circuit
•ECM

DTC Troubleshooting
Step Action Yes No

Was “Engine and Emission Control System Check” performed?

2
Oil pressure switch-2 signal circuit check
1)Set ignition “OFF” and disconnect “D01” connector from ECM and oil pressure switch-2 connector.
2)Check for proper terminal connection to “D01” connector and oil pressure switch-2 connector.
3)If connections are OK, check the following points.
•Resistance of “C1” circuit: less than 1 Ω
•Resistance between “C1” and ground: infinity
•Voltage of “C1” circuit: approx. 0 V (When ignition is “ON”)
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 3.
Repair or replace defective wire harness.
3
Oil pressure switch-2 check
1)Check oil pressure switch-2.  
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 4.
Replace oil pressure switch-2.  
4
VDOP solenoid valve power supply circuit and drive circuit check
1)Check VDOP solenoid valve power supply circuit and drive circuit according to Step 2 – 3 of DTC P1540:D16AA.
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 5.
Repair or replace defective wire harness.

VDOP solenoid valve check
1)Check VDOP solenoid valve.
