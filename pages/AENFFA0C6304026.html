Troubleshooting
Step Action Yes No
1
Circuit fuse check
1)Check that ignition is “OFF”.
2)Disconnect “G55” and “G50” connectors.
3)Check for proper connection to “G50-3” and “G55-1” terminals.
4)If connections are OK, check “P/S” fuse and “IG1 SIG” fuse for blowing.
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 2.
Replace fuse(s) and check for short to ground in circuits connected to fuse(s).
2
P/S control module power supply circuit check
1)Check voltage between “G55-1” terminal and ground.
Is voltage 10 – 14 V?
Go to Step 3.
“A1” circuit is open.
3
IG1 power supply circuit check
1)Set ignition “ON”.
2)Check voltage between “G50-3” terminal and ground.
Is voltage 10 – 14 V?
Go to Step 4.
“A2” circuit is open.
4
P/S control module ground circuit check
1)Set ignition “OFF”.
2)Check for proper connection to “G55-2” terminal.
3)Check resistance between “G55-2” terminal and ground.
Is resistance 1 Ω or less?
End.
“A3” circuit is open or too high in resistance.
