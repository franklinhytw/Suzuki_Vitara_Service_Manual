2B
Coil Spring, Strut Bearing and Strut Support Check
Reference: Front Strut Assembly Disassembly and Reassembly
•Check coil spring for weakening, crack, deformation and damage.
•Check bump stopper, strut dust cover, coil spring upper seat, coil spring lower seat and strut support for crack, deformation and damage.
•Check strut bearing for wear, abnormal noise and damage.
If check result is not OK, replace defective parts with new ones.
