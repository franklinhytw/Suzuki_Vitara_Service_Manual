3A
Rear Drive Shaft Assembly Removal and Installation
Reference: Rear Drive Shaft Components:Rear
Reference: Rear Drive Shaft Assembly On-Vehicle Inspection:Rear
Removal
1)Hoist vehicle and remove rear wheel.
2)Release caulking from drive shaft nut (1) and remove drive shaft nut with brake pedal depressed.
3)Give matchmark (1) on joint housing of rear drive shaft assembly and drive shaft flange of rear differential as shown.
4)Remove rear drive shaft flange nuts (2), and then remove rear drive shaft assembly (3).
Installation
Reverse removal procedure noting the following points.
•Align matchmark (1) on joint housing of rear drive shaft assembly and drive shaft flange of rear differential.
•Temporarily tighten new drive shaft nut, and then tighten drive shaft flange nuts (2) to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Drive shaft flange nut (a): 50 N·m (5.1 kgf-m, 37.0 lbf-ft)
•Tighten drive shaft nut (1) to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Drive shaft nut (a): 175 N·m (17.8 kgf-m, 129.0 lbf-ft)
•Caulk drive shaft nut (1) as shown.
2. Caulking part
•Install rear wheel.
