Engine Symptom Diagnosis
Perform troubleshooting referring to the followings when ECM has detected no DTC and no abnormality has been found in Visual Inspection:D16AA and Engine Basic Inspection:D16AA.
Condition Correction / Reference Item
Hard starting (Engine can be cranked) Refer to Hard Starting (Engine Can Be Cranked).
Engine not running smoothly Refer to Engine Not Running Smoothly.
Engine does not produce best performance Refer to Engine Does Not Produce Best Performance.
Engine hesitates-momentary lack of response as accelerator is depressed Refer to Engine Hesitates – Momentary Lack of Response as Accelerator Is Depressed.
Improper engine idle or engine stops at idle Refer to Improper Engine Idling or Engine Stops at Idle.

Engine knocking Refer to Engine Knocking.
White exhaust fumes Refer to White Exhaust Fumes.
Black exhaust fumes Refer to Black Exhaust Fumes.
Excessive exhaust fumes Refer to Excessive Exhaust Fumes.

Hard Starting (Engine Can Be Cranked)
Step Action Yes No
1
Preliminary check
1)Check the following points.
•Check that engine oil level is correct.  
•Check that air intake / turbocharger circuit is working properly.  
•Check that main and circuit fuses have not blown.
•Check fuel heater.  
•Wiring on engine correctly connected to ground.
•Battery correctly connected to ground.
•Battery positive (+) cable and all leads are firmly connected.
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 2.
Repair or replace defective part(s).
2
Environmental parameter check
1)When ignition is “OFF”, connect SUZUKI scan tool to DLC.

5
Cylinder head gasket and valve oil seal check
1)Check the following points.
•Check that cylinder head is correctly tightened
•Check seal of cylinder head gasket
•Check seal of valve oil seals
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 6.
•Cylinder head tightening incorrect:
—Proceed with removing-refitting of cylinder head to correctly retighten cylinder head.  
•Cylinder head gasket damaged:
—Change cylinder head gasket.  
•Valve oil seal missing:
—Replace valve oil seals.  
6
Blow-by gas intake circuit check
1)Check condition of blow-by gas intake circuit, especially for obstructions in separator or hoses.
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 7.
•Fault in hose between oil separator and intake manifold:
—Restore circuit to working order.
—Change pipe if necessary.
•Fault in oil separator:
—Restore circuit to working order.
—If necessary, replace cylinder head cover.
7
Turbocharger seal check
1)Check that there is no engine oil leakage from turbocharger impeller shaft.
Is check result OK?
End.
Gasket seal insufficient:
•Replace turbocharger.

Cylinder head gasket and valve oil seal check
1)Check the following points.
•Check that cylinder head is correctly tightened.
•Check seal of cylinder head gasket.
•Check seal of stem seals.
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 4.
•Cylinder head tightening incorrect:
—Proceed with removing-refitting of cylinder head to correctly retighten cylinder head.  
•Cylinder head gasket damaged:
—Change cylinder head gasket.  
•Valve oil seal missing:
—Replace stem seals.  
4
Blow-by gas intake circuit check
1)Check condition of blow-by gas intake circuit, especially for obstructions in separator or hoses.
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 5.
•Fault in hose between oil separator and intake manifold:
—Restore circuit to working order.
—Change pipe if necessary.
•Fault in oil separator:
—Restore circuit to working order.
—If necessary, replace cylinder head cover.
