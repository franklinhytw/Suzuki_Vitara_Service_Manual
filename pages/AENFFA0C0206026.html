Clutch Fluid Replacement (M/T Model)
NOTE:
Clutch fluid is supplied from brake fluid reservoir.
Change clutch fluid as follows.
Drain existing fluid from clutch system completely, fill system with specified fluid and carry out air bleeding operation.
For air bleeding procedure, refer to “Air Bleeding of Clutch System” in Section 5C.
