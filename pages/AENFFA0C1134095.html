DTC P2562 / P2563
DTC Detecting Condition and Trouble Area
DTC detecting condition Trouble area
P2562: Turbocharger Boost Control Position Sensor “A” Circuit
Output voltage of turbocharger actuator position sensor signal circuit is higher than specified value.
(1 D/C detection logic)
•Turbocharger actuator position sensor and/or its circuit
•ECM
P2563: Turbocharger Boost Control Position Sensor “A” Circuit Range / Performance
Any of the following conditions is met.
•Difference between measured turbocharger position and targeted turbocharger position is more than specified value.
•Output voltage of turbocharger actuator position sensor signal circuit is higher than specified value.
(1 D/C detection logic)
