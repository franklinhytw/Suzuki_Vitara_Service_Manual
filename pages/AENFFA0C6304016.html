C1153: Power Supply Voltage Too Low
Battery voltage is lower than 9 V even if one of the following conditions is met:
•Voltage at fail-safe relay contact is higher than 19.2 V.
•Engine speed is more than 600 rpm and fail-safe relay ON command is outputted.
•Charging system
•P/S control module (steering column assembly)
C1155: P/S Control Module Internal Failure / Power Supply Voltage Too High
P/S control module internal failure.
Circuit Diagram

A1: P/S control module power supply circuit 2. CPU 5. To continuous power supply
A2: P/S control module ground circuit 3. Main fuse box    
DTC Confirmation Procedure
Run engine at idle speed for more than 5 seconds.
