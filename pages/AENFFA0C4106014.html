Brake Flexible Hose and Pipe Inspection
Reference: Brake Flexible Hose and Pipe Removal and Installation
Flexible Hose
Check the following points. If check result is not OK, replace brake flexible hose.
•Connection for leakage and other damage
•Blister, crack and other damage
Pipe
Check brake pipe for damage, crack, dents and corrosion. If check result is not OK, replace pipe.
