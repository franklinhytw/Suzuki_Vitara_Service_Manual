9H
Trim Removal and Installation
Removal
Front pillar trim
Remove front pillar trim (1) pulling it in arrow direction.
Dash side trim and front side sill scuff
Remove front side sill scuff (1) and then remove dash side trim (2) pulling them in arrow directions.
Rear side sill scuff
Remove rear side sill scuff (1) pulling it in arrow direction.
Center pillar trim
1)Remove front and rear side sill scuffs (4).
2)Remove center pillar lower trim (1) pulling it in arrow direction.
3)Remove shoulder adjuster cover (5).
4)Remove shoulder adjuster bolt (3).
5)Remove center pillar upper trim (2) pulling it in arrow directions.
Tail end trim
Remove clips and then remove tail end trim (1) pulling it in arrow direction.
Quarter inner trim
1)Remove rear side sill scuff (1) and tail end trim (2).
2)Remove rear seat.  
3)Remove hook, clips and bolts, and then remove rear quarter inner lower trim (3) pulling it in arrow direction.
4)Remove rear quarter inner upper trim (4) pulling it in arrow direction.
Installation
Reverse removal procedure noting the following points.
•Tighten shoulder adjuster bolt to specified torque.  
•Tighten rear seat hinge bolt to specified torque.
