Water Pump / Generator Drive Belt Removal and Installation
Removal
1)Disconnect negative (–) cable at battery.
2)Remove engine undercover.
3)Remove compressor drive belt before removing water pump / generator drive belt (1).  
4)Loosen generator bolt (2), pivot bolt (3) and adjuster bolt (4), and then remove water pump / generator drive belt.
5)Remove water pump / generator drive belt tensioner pulley if necessary.  
Installation
1)Install water pump / generator drive belt tensioner pulley, if removed.  
2)Install water pump / generator drive belt (1) as shown in figure.
3)Adjust belt tension.  
4)Install compressor drive belt.  
NOTE:
Check that drive belt is installed in grooves of each pulley correctly.
5)Install engine undercover.
6)Connect negative (–) cable at battery.
