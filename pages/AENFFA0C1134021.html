DTC Detecting Condition and Trouble Area
DTC detection condition Trouble area
P0102: Mass or Volume Air Flow “A” Circuit Low
Measured MAF is lower than specified value.
(3 D/C detection logic)
•MAF sensor and/or its circuit
•Barometric pressure sensor (in ECM)
•ECM
P0103: Mass or Volume Air Flow “A” Circuit High
Measured MAF is higher than specified value.
(3 D/C detection logic)
P010F: Mass or Volume Air Flow Sensor A / B Correlation
Calculated ratio measured MAF value and standard MAF value is out of specified range for specified time.
(1 D/C detection logic)
