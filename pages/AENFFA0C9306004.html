9C
Horn Removal and Installation
Removal
1)Remove front bumper.  
2)Remove cap (3) and then disconnect horn connector (1).
3)Remove horn (2) from front bumper member.
Installation
Reference: Horn Inspection
Reverse removal procedure noting the following points.
•Tighten horn bolt to specified torque.
Tightening torque
Horn bolt (a): 14 N·m (1.4 kgf-m, 10.5 lbf-ft)
