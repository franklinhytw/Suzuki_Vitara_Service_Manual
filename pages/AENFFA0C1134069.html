DTC Detecting Condition and Trouble Area
DTC detecting condition Trouble area
P0A5A: Generator Current Sensor Circuit Range / Performance
Any of the following conditions is met.
•Output voltage of electric load current sensor signal circuit is more than specified value.
•Output voltage of electric load current sensor signal circuit is lower than specified value.
•Output voltage of electric load current sensor signal circuit is out of specified range.
(1 D/C detection logic but MIL does not light up)
•Electric load current sensor and/or its circuit
•ECM
•ENG A-STOP control module (ENG A-STOP model)

4
Electric load current sensor signal circuit check
1)With ignition is “OFF”, disconnect “E01” connector from ECM.
2)Check for proper terminal connection to “E01” connector.
3)If connections are OK, check the following points.
•Resistance of “A2” circuit: less than 1 Ω
•Resistance between “A2” circuit and ground: infinity
•Resistance between “A2” circuit terminal and other terminal at electric load current sensor connector: infinity
•Voltage of “A2” circuit: approx. 0 V (When ignition is “ON”)
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 8.
Repair or replace defective wire harness.
