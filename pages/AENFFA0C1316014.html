CMP Sensor On-Vehicle Inspection
1)With ignition “OFF”, disconnect CMP sensor connector.
2)Connect service wires (1) between CMP sensor and CMP sensor connector as shown in figure.
3)With ignition “ON”, check that output voltage varies depending on position of signal rotor as follows.
[A]: Approx. 5 V
CMP sensor is facing to projection of signal rotor.
[B]: Approx. 0 V
CMP sensor is not facing to projection of signal rotor.
