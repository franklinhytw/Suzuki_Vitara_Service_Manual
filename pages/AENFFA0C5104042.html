P2719: Pressure Control Solenoid “D” Control Circuit Range / Performance
Difference between measured circuit current of pressure control solenoid valve “D” and commanded circuit current is higher than specified value.
(1 D/C detection logic)
•Pressure control solenoid valve “D”
•Solenoid harness with ATF temperature sensor
•TCM
P2720: Pressure Control Solenoid “D” Control Circuit Low
Pressure control solenoid valve “D” drive circuit is open or shorted to ground.
(1 D/C detection logic)
P2721: Pressure Control Solenoid “D” Control Circuit High
Pressure control solenoid valve “D” drive circuit is shorted to power supply.
(1 D/C detection logic)

3
Solenoid harness check
1)Remove transaxle side oil pan.  
2)Disconnect pressure control solenoid valve “D” connector.
3)Check for proper terminal connections to solenoid harness connector and pressure control solenoid valve “D” connector.
4)If connections are OK, check the following points.
•Resistance of “A1” and “A2” circuits: less than 1 Ω
•Resistance between each of “A1” and “A2” circuits and ground: infinity
•Resistance between “A1” circuit terminal and other terminal at solenoid harness connector: infinity
•Resistance between “A2” circuit terminal and other terminal at solenoid harness connector: infinity
•Voltage of “A1” and “A2” circuits: approx. 0 V (When ignition is “ON”)
Is check result OK?
Go to Step 4.
Replace solenoid harness with ATF temperature sensor.

Pressure control solenoid valve “D” check

Is check result OK?
